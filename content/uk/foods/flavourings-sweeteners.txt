Flavourings & Sweeteners
========================

category

foods/flavourings-sweeteners

**Bulk™** supplies a range of Flavourings & Sweeteners. This range was primarily designed to complement the fact that we sell a huge range of unflavoured and unsweetened products, enabling users to flavour and sweeten to their own taste. From powerful sweeteners such as Stevia and Sucralose, to our unique, zero-calorie flavouring system LiquiFlav™ – this range of Flavourings & Sweeteners can be used to enhance anything from Protein Shakes to Porridge.
