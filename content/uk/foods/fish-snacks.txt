Fish Snacks
===========

category

foods/fish-snacks

Our **fish snacks** add a whole new dimension to current snacking habits, which is consuming high quality fish on-the-go! **Fish Jerky** is based on the same idea as Beef Jerky, a popular high protein snack which is ultra-convenient.

The macronutrient content of **Fish Jerky** is exceptional. It is higher in protein than a standard Beef Jerky, as well as lower in saturated fat and salt. It is also a great source of Omega 3.

**Fish Jerky** is a tasty savoury alternative to your normal whey protein drink and would be a perfect snack for anyone looking to increase their protein intake at any time of the day.
