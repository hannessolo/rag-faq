Clear Protein
=============

category

Protein for juice lovers. Clear protein is an innovative [protein powder](/uk/protein) that combines fruity flavours with high-protein content. It's a protein shake with a juice-like flavour and consistency.

Made through hydrolysation, [Clear Whey Isolate](/uk/products/clear-whey-isolate/bpb-cwhe) is a juice-like protein shake, offering an alternative to creamy shakes such as whey protein and vegan protein powder. Perfect for squash drinkers.

Easy to mix, easy to drink, shake up your supplement stack with [Clear Whey All In One](/uk/products/clear-all-in-one/bble-cwa1) or [Clear Vegan Protein](/uk/products/clear-vegan-protein/vser-cveg).
