### Complete Protein Blend™

Our easy mixing, comprehensive high protein formula for anyone looking to add more protein to their diet. Loaded with a huge 23g of protein in a 30g serving, available in 7 delicious flavours.

Our Complete Protein Blend™ is a high quality, premium multi-protein blend made with whey protein and milk protein, ideal if you want to combine fast and slow release protein sources to ensure you get a steady protein supply throughout your day.

Our Complete Protein Blend™ is packed with all the essential protein you need in a single delicious shake. By using a combination of fast and slow release protein sources, your body gets a regular intake of protein from the moment you drink it, and we’ve even added some glutamine peptides for an extra nutritional boost. Each serving gives you 23g of protein as well as the complete collection of amino acids.

Directions
----------

### Directions

Mix 1 slightly heaped scoop (approximately 30g) with 200ml water in a shaker bottle.

We recommend 1-3 servings per day, but in accordance with your own protein requirements.

You could enjoy a shake in the morning, mix into a smoothie or stir into porridge. Suitable any time of day to increase your protein intake, particularly as part of a healthy lifestyle.
