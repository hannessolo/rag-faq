Glucomannan\* has been shown to increase the feeling of fullness and contribute to weight                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |

|                | With over 4g glucomannan per 200g pack, these could be your stir-fry saviour. Stir into soups, toss into salads, mix in some sauce or simply season as a side. Short of time? They’re ready in just one minute. If you’re looking to lighten the load, this is the perfect switch for carb-based noodles.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      |
