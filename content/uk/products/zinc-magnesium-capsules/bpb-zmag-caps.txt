### Zinc & Magnesium Capsules

ZINC & MAGNESIUM: IN ONE EASY DOSE

Looking to add more zinc and magnesium to your diet? Our Zinc & Magnesium Capsules are ideal for anyone looking to supplement their regular diet. One dose will provide you with 20mg of zinc and 300mg of magnesium.

WHY SHOULD I TAKE IT?

Magnesium brings many benefits:

*   Contributes to a reduction of tiredness and fatigue
*   Contributes to normal protein synthesis and energy yielding metabolism (aka generating energy from nutrients)
*   Essential for the maintenance of normal teeth and bones

Zinc also comes with its own benefits:

*   It contributes to the maintenance of normal testosterone levels in the blood
*   Supplementing Zinc also contributes to normal cognitive function
