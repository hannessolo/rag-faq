### Protein Balls

Meet our protein balls - nutritious, soft and a source of plant protein, allowing you to make the sustainable choice without compromising on flavour, texture or nutrition.

Made up of a few simple high quality ingredients, including dates, nuts and plant protein rolled into bite sized balls, these are perfect for on the go snacking. Each bag contains at least 8g of protein, no added sugars\* and offers a source of fibre. Upgrade your healthy snacking game by allowing these balls to roll into your life and fuel your workouts.
