Description

### Complete Fruits™

This premium blend combines fruit powders, freeze-dried fruits and red foods into a nutritional, naturally-flavoured superfruit powder.

Our Complete Fruits™ powder is a delicious and naturally flavoured blend of 10 superfruit ingredients that’s perfect for pimping up smoothies and shakes. Low in fat and high in fibre, it’s also a great way to add fruits and red foods into a healthy, balanced lifestyle.

Created exclusively for **Bulk™**, our Complete Fruits™ powder combines acai berry, lucuma, baobab, beetroot, banana, lime juice, raspberry, strawberry, blueberry and grape seed into a delicious superfruit blend.

\\

### Purpose

Complete Fruits™ is perfect for anyone looking to increase their fibre intake, and add fruit-based nutrition to their diet in one convenient powder. It’s especially great for adding to your favourite protein-enhanced smoothies, or using to sweeten yoghurts or porridge.
