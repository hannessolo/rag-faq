### Directions

Mix 3.5 scoops (approximately 12.5g) with 250ml water in a shaker bottle. Consume 30 mins before exercise, ideally on an empty stomach, 1 serving per day.

**WARNINGS:**  High caffeine content (80mg/100ml). Not recommended for children, or pregnant or breastfeeding women.

Contains silica bag - DO NOT EAT.

This product contains Beta Alanine which may produce a tingling, itching or flushing sensation of the skin. This effect is harmless and typically subsides within an hour of use. Read this [blogpost](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/why-does-pre-workout-make-you-itch/)to learn more.
