### Ginkgo Biloba Tablets 6000mg

Super-concentrated Ginkgo biloba packed into a convenient tablet form, providing 120mg of 50:1 Ginkgo biloba extract, equivalent to 6000mg of whole leaves.

Ginkgo biloba, also known as the maidenhair tree, is an ancient tree native to China, estimated to have existed for over 270 million years. The leaves of the Ginkgo biloba tree have been used in traditional Chinese medicine for centuries, and have become a focus of research in recent years for their active compounds, flavone glycosides and terpene lactones.

Our Ginkgo Biloba Tablets provide 120mg of super-concentrated 50:1 Ginkgo biloba per tablet, equivalent to 6000mg of whole leaves.
