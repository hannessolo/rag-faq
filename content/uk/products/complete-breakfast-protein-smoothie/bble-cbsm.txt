### Breakfast Smoothie

Our delicious, high protein breakfast smoothie to kickstart your morning, made with real fruit pieces and natural freeze-dried powder, delivering an impressive 24g of protein and 3.5g of fibre per serving.

Our Breakfast Smoothie™ is a fruity, high protein drink available in two delicious flavours. Containing high quality whey protein, premium ultra fine oats, DigeZyme®; digestive enzymes and psyllium husk powder, it’s an ideal choice for anyone looking for a fruity protein source to start their day.

We used natural fruit powders and freeze-dried fruit pieces to ensIure the fruits in this formula retain their natural goodness, while ultra fine oats add a low GI source of carbohydrates for a slow energy release. We’ve also added a dose of DigeZyme®, a proprietary blend of 5 digestive enzymes that aid digestion and breakdown of protein, carbohydrate, fat and lactose. It’s available in delicious Blueberry & Raspberry or Strawberry & Banana flavours, and can even be combined with ourZero Calorie Syrup™ for an extra burst of flavour.

### Directions

Mix 2.5 scoops (approx. 60g) with 400ml of cold water in a **Bulk™** Shaker Bottle and shake well. Use at breakfast or any time of day for a healthy calorie boost. Consume 1-2 servings daily.
