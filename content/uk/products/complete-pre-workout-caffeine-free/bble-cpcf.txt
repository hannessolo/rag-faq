### Pre-Workout Caffeine Free

This stimulant-free version of our popular Complete Pre-Workout™ gives you all the benefits to boost your training session without the caffeine overload. Packed with over 13g of active ingredients per serving, including 3g arginine alpha-ketoglutarate (AAKG), 3g beta alanine, 3g creatine monohydrate and 3g citrulline malate.

Our Pre-Workout Caffeine Free drink has been expertly formulated to kickstart your training session with zero stimulants. Each serving provides optimal doses of key nutrients to support you through your workout.

We modified our Complete Pre-Workout™ formula by removing any stimulants and adding a range of alternative pre-workout ingredients, as well as increasing the dosage of several key nutrients. The result is a fully loaded, stimulant-free pre-workout drink that prepares you to push harder through every set.

\* Creatine has been shown to increase physical performance in successive bursts of short-term, high-intensity exercise.

\*\* Vitamin B6 contributes to reduction in tiredness and fatigue, as well as supporting normal psychological function.

Directions

### Directions

Mix 3 slightly heaped scoops (approx. 14.5 g) with 250ml of cold water in a shaker bottle and shake well. Consume 30-45 minutes prior to training, ideally on an empty stomach, use one serving per day.

Contains silica bag - DO NOT EAT.

**WARNING:** This product contains Beta Alanine which may produce a tingling, itching or flushing sensation of the skin. This effect is harmless and typically subsides within an hour of use. Read this [blogpost](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/why-does-pre-workout-make-you-itch/)to learn more.
