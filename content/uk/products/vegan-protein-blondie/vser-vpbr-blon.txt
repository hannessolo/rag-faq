### Vegan Protein Blondie

A delicious, high protein vegan blondie delivering premium nutrition with amazing flavour. Each blondie is packed with 16g of protein, making them an ideal for snacking on the go.

Made with real sprinkles, our Vegan Protein blondie is a soft, sweet high-protein treat. Rich in protein and a great source of fibre, they’re great for hitting your daily protein target without any artificial sweeteners.

\- A great alternative source of protein to shakes

\- Soft-baked for an irresistible texture

\- Made with real sprinkles

Our Vegan Protein blondies are perfect for anyone with a vegan diet looking for an alternative or additional source of protein as part of their training journey, or anyone looking for a delicious and guilt-free snack.

### Directions

Consume a Vegan Protein blondie whenever a high protein snack is required, or in accordance with your dietary requirements. Consume a maximum of two blondies per day. Excessive consumption may produce a laxative effect.
