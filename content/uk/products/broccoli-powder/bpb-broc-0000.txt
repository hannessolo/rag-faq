Description

### Broccoli Powder

All the goodness of broccoli, provided in a fine powder form, creating a nutrient rich, high fibre ingredient that’s perfect for adding to smoothies.

Made only from fresh, whole broccoli, our unique low temperature dehydration process means that our Broccoli Powder has all the goodness of fresh broccoli. High in fibre, it can be used for cooking, baking, or added to homemade smoothies to increase the nutritional content.

Our high-quality, pure Broccoli Powder is derived entirely from both the stalks and florets of fresh broccoli, and is manufactured via a low temperature dehydration process that maintains the nutritional integrity of the broccoli. It’s also packed with an impressive 32g of fibre per 100g.

### Purpose

Our Broccoli Powder is perfect for anyone looking for a concentrated form of broccoli to add to their diet, and is especially suitable for adding to homemade smoothies for extra nutritional content.
