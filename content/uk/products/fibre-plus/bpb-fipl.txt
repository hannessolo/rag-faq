### Fibre Plus

#### Fibre power in every scoop

Hitting bigger protein goals to hit the gym harder? Then you need to add more fibre to your diet. That’s where Fibre Plus comes in — your daily gut health\*\* booster.

Two servings deliver more than ⅓ of your daily recommended fibre intake\*\*\*, combining soluble and insoluble fibre from natural sources like chicory inulin, psyllium husk, flaxseed, and apple fibre.

Fueling your body the right way, hitting your fibre needs, and being at your peak has never been easier.

Directions

### Directions

#### How do I use it?

Mix two heaped scoops (approximately 8 g) in 300 ml water or add to your favourite smoothie. We recommend starting with 1 serving and gradually increasing to 2 servings based on your preference.

#### When should I use it?

Fibre Plus is best used as part of your morning routine.
