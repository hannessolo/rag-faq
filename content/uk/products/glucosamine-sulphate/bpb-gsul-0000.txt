### Glucosamine Sulphate Powder

100% premium glucosamine sulphate powder, providing high potency glucosamine amino sugar in an easy-to-mix form.

Glucosamine is an amino sugar commonly found in the shells of shellfish, and can be found in high levels in the fluid surrounding joints in the body. It’s also a popular supplement in sports nutrition formulas. Our Glucosamine Sulphate comes in a convenient powder that is a great way to mix glucosamine into your daily nutritional intake.

Each serving of our Glucosamine Sulphate provides 1000mg of pure glucosamine sulphate – ideal for anyone looking to add glucosamine into their dietary intake. We recommend mixing it into your regular protein or workout formula.
