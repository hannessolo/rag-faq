### Taurine

100% pure taurine in a convenient powder form, providing the popular energy drink ingredient in a convenient form for mixing into your pre-workout and thermogenic blends.

Taurine is a well known ingredient used by many high street energy drink brands. Our taurine powder offers unbeatable value for money, and is a great addition to pre-workout shakes or smoothies for a little extra boost.

Taurine is a naturally occurring amino acid commonly found in skeletal muscle, and as well as being found in foods such as fish, meat, and dairy. It’s typically taken in combination with caffeine, BCAAs, and thermogenic ingredients such as black pepper extract.
