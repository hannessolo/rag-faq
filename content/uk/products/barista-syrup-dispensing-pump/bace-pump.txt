### Barista Syrup Dispensing Pump

Looking to perfect your portion control? Pouring a big bottle onto a tiny spoon isn’t the easiest manoeuvre. This little product is sure to get you feeling pumped.

Simple but effective, our dispensing pump has been specially designed to fit our Zero Calorie Barista Syrup. Shaking it up, adding sweetness to coffee or drizzling onto a dessert. Get set to measure up every time.

Insert the dispensing pump into the bottle and screw it in. Rotate the head of the pump, then press down to dispense one or more shots of syrup. It can be reused, just make sure you clean thoroughly before switching bottles.
