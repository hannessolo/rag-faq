### Dried Goji Berries - 500g

100% dried goji berries, a convenient and delicious source of protein in fruit form, perfect for snacking on the go or as an extra source of nutrition between meals.

Goji berries, also known as wolfberries, are a delicious small, red fruit. These Dried Goji Berries are a convenient snack for when you’re on the move, or for mixing into smoothies, or even home baking recipes.

Fruit typically contains low amounts of protein, but our goji berries contains 12g of protein per 100g and are additive and preservative free.

### Purpose

Our Dried Goji Berries are perfect for anyone looking for an easy and delicious fruit-based snack at any time of day. Edible straight from the pack, they’re a great option for anyone who wants to increase their protein consumption to help growth and maintenance of muscle mass, as well as for adding to meals to boost nutrition and add some natural flavour.
