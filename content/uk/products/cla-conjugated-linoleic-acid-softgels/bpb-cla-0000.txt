### Conjugated Linoleic Acid Softgels

A potent dose of the omega 6 fatty acid linoleic acid in a convenient softgel form, providing 80% active isomers including c9t11 and c10t12.

Conjugated linoleic acid, or CLA, is a modified form of the omega 6 fatty acid linoleic acid, a natural fatty acid that cannot be produced by the body. These 800mg softgels provide you with a potent dose of CLA to supplement your diet and ensure you’re getting the nutrition you need.

CLA has a general structure of polyunsaturated linoleic acid, containing a mixture of fatty acid isomers. Our CLA Softgels contain 80% active isomers, specifically 370mg of c9t11 and 370mg of c10t12, providing an easy way to supplement your required dose of CLA in a convenient softgel capsule.
