### Bedtime Protein Shake

A delicious nighttime protein shake formula, using slow-digesting micellar casein to deliver 30g of protein in a single serving, along with a range of other key nutrients and amino acids to nourish your body through the night.

Our Bedtime Shake is a great source of slow-digesting protein with an impressive 86% protein content. We supplemented its slow-digesting protein with leucine, HMB, theanine, and lysine to create an all-round, easy mixing, great tasting formula that’s perfect for drinking at night.

Our Bedtime Shake is an optimally-dosed and delicious way to finish your day, made with premium source slow-digesting micellar casein protein. It’s also incredibly low in both fat and sugar, with less than 1g of fat or carbohydrates per serving. As always, we’ve included each ingredient at research-proven levels to provide you optimal nutrition, whatever the time of day.
