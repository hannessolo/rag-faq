### Beef Jerky

Premium beef jerky manufactured exclusively for **Bulk™**. Rich in protein, using only the best cuts of Silverside beef to ensure the highest quality, available in 2 delicious flavours.

Our high quality Beef Jerky is a delicious and convenient protein rich snack, providing at least 47g of protein in a single 100g bag, equivalent to 250g of premium, tender, and lean Silverside beef. Every pack is sourced from grass-fed cows, and is fully traceable. And best of all, it’s the perfect snack for paleo diets.

Our Beef Jerky is available in two flavours: delicious Original and Sweet Chilli for anyone who likes an extra kick with their protein. Every pack is slowly air-dried to perfection, and free from colourings and flavourings.

Our Beef Jerky is suitable for anyone looking for a high protein savoury snack at any time of the day. It’s a winning solution for reaching your daily protein target without unnecessary calories, especially if you’re on a paleo diet.
