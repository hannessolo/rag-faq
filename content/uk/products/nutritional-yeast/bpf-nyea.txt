### Nutritional Yeast

Nutritional Yeast may not sound the tastiest option to the uninitiated. However, these powerful little flakes offer a lot more than meets the eye. They are ideal for vegetarians and vegans, providing a natural source of protein and a whole host of B-Vitamins.

Our Nutritional Yeast is suitable for all sorts of cooking and baking. It can be sprinkled over salads and pasta to add flavour, incorporated into soups or even used in something as luxurious as a vegan mac n’ cheese! The flakes are an incredibly versatile, convenient and tasty way of adding protein, fibre, vitamins and minerals to your food.

These tasty little flakes provide 49g protein and 27g fibre per 100g, along with a source of Thiamin, Niacin, Folic Acid and Phosphorus. Our Nutritional Yeast is made in Europe and is supplied in plastic-free, home-compostable packaging.
