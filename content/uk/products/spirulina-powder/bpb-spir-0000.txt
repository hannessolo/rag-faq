### Spirulina Powder

100% pure spirulina powder, providing 63% protein content.

Spirulina is an algae that typically grows in natural alkaline lakes in tropical and subtropical climates. Consumed as a food for hundreds of years, it’s experienced rising popularity in recent years for its nutritional profile. As a plant-based complete protein source, it’s an ideal protein supplement for vegans and vegetarians.

A complete protein source is a food that contains all 9 of the essential amino acids your body needs in your daily diet. Most plant-based protein sources typically lack one or two amino acids, making Spirulina particularly impressive. Put simply, our Spirulina Powder is an ideal nutritional supplement if you want a plant-based protein source to complete your nutritional intake.
