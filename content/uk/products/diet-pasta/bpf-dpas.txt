### Diet Pasta 200g

Our low calorie pasta alternative with 2.3g of glucomannan per 100g, providing the perfect meal accompaniment for athletes on an energy restricted diet.

Our Diet Pasta is made from popular weight loss ingredient glucomannan, which is a water soluble fibre sourced from the Amorphophalli konjac plant. Unlike normal pasta, this contains just 10 calories, 2.8g of fibre and absolutely no fat whatsoever, making it the perfect meal when compared to many carbohydrate based foods.

Glucomannan has been shown to contribute to weight loss\* in the context of an energy restricted diet, as well as contributing to the maintenance of normal blood cholesterol levels\*\*.

\\

### Purpose

Our Diet Pasta is the perfect low calorie addition to meals. Providing 2.8g of glucomannan per 100g and lower in calories, carbohydrates and fat compared to regular pasta, Diet Pasta is ideal for anyone wanting to feel fuller throughout the day on an energy restricted diet. Ready in 1 minute, it’s versatility means it can be used as a low calorie side dish with chicken or fish, or even added to soups and stir fries.

\*Diet pasta contains Glucomannan which contributes to weight loss in the context of an energy restricted diet. The beneficial effect is obtained with a daily intake of diet pasta in 3 doses of 44g each, taken with 1-2 glasses of water before meals.

\*\*Glucomannan contributes to the maintenance of normal blood cholesterol levels. The beneficial effect is obtained with a daily intake of 4g of glucomannan.

Directions
----------

### Directions

**1.** Drain away excess fluid and rinse 2-3 times. **2.** Leave in hot water for 1 minute. **3.** Drain (pat dry extra moisture with a paper towel). **4.** Add to stir fry, soups and salads.

**WARNING**: Consume with adequate fluid intake. There is a risk of choking for people with swallowing difficulties or when ingesting with inadequate fluid intake.
