### Vegan Protein Pancake Mix

Whipping up American-style pancakes couldn’t be simpler. Our improved Vegan Protein Pancake Mix recipe has been refined to give you showstopping pancakes in minutes. We’ve blended brown rice protein, pea protein and quinoa flour to create light, fluffy pancakes. And with an impressive 17g protein per serving, you’re a step towards your nutrition goals. Ready to mix up a masterpiece?

Choose from Lemon, Maple Syrup and Vanilla flavours. Enjoy as a high-protein breakfast, a mid-afternoon snack or a tasty dessert to curb those sweet cravings.

We’ve worked hard on the recipe so you don’t have to. Just add water, pour into a pan and you’re ready to flip. It’s time to stack and serve with your favourite toppings. Our Zero Calorie Syrup™ or Peanut Butter make the perfect partners. You choose.

Directions
----------

### Directions

1.  Place 2 scoops (approximately 50g) of powder into a mixing bowl and add 70ml - 80ml of water, depending on how thick you like your pancakes, and stir until fully mixed.
2.  Heat a little oil in a non-stick frying pan.
3.  Using a spoon, drop a large spoonful of the mixture into the pan and cook on a medium-high heat for approximately 2-3 minutes on each side until lightly browned.
4.  Serve with your favourite pancake toppings.
