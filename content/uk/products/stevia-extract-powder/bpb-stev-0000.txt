### Stevia Extract Powder

100% pure stevia powder, a natural sweetener made from a highly purified stevia extract that’s 400 times sweeter than sugar – perfect for cutting down your carb intake.

Manufactured exclusively for **Bulk**™, our Stevia Extract Powder is a highly purified and 97% pure Reb A version of the natural sweetener stevia. 400 times sweeter than sugar and highly soluble, it can be used to sweeten almost anything without adding unnecessary sugars to your diet.

Our Stevia Extract Powder is a high intensity natural sweetener made from the leaves of the Stevia rebaudiana plant. In contrast to the majority of the stevia products on the market that are often mixed with maltodextrin, our Stevia Extract Powder is 100% pure stevia extract, made from highly purified rebaudioside A (Reb A) with no unnecessary additives or fillers.
