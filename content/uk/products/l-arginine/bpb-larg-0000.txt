Description

### L Arginine

100% pure l-arginine powder, a conditionally essential amino acid and popular pre-workout ingredient. Unflavoured for easy mixing, each 3g serving provides 3g of pure l-arginine.

Our L-Arginine powder is as simple as it sounds. 100% pure l-arginine in a simple, convenient powder form that you can mix into your pre-workout formulas for a nutritional hit of amino acids.

L-Arginine is a conditionally essential amino acid that can be obtained by the diet, but sometimes your body’s requirement for arginine can be greater than what it produces – that’s why it’s conditionally essential.

\\

### Purpose

Our L-Arginine is a perfect ingredient to add to your pre-workout formula if you want an extra hit of amino acid before you train, or even for making your own personal formula. We’d recommend blending it with caffeine, beta alanine, citrulline malate and branched chain amino acids.

Directions
----------

### Directions

Mix 1 scoop (approximately 3g) with 200ml water in a shaker bottle. Take 30 minutes before exercise, ideally on an empty stomach, 1-2 servings per day.
