### Directions

### How do I use it?

Mix 1 slightly heaped scoop (approximately 17g) with 250ml water in a shaker bottle. Bulk Protip: add the water first to achieve the perfect ratio.

Consume 30 minutes before exercise, ideally on an empty stomach. Enjoy 1 serving per day.

WARNING: Contains caffeine. Not recommended for children or pregnant women (200mg caffeine per daily 17g serving). Contains silica bag - DO NOT EAT.

This product contains beta alanine which may produce a tingling, itching or flushing sensation of the skin. This effect is harmless and typically subsides within an hour of use.
