### Keto Coffee

A ready-to-drink coffee combining Colombian Fairtrade Coffee, MCTs and collagen peptides. All this in a convenient hot drink providing over 7g of MCTs, 11g of protein and 100mg caffeine per serving.

Our Keto Coffee is the perfect hot drink for anyone following the popular ketogenic diet, requiring higher fat and lower sugar consumption. Our formula provides all your necessary protein nutrition with a beneficial amount of fat to support you on your keto journey. For those with a sweet tooth, try our Keto Mocha for the same benefits of our Keto Coffee with a delicious hint of chocolate.

Our Keto Coffee takes seconds to prepare, tastes great and will fit seamlessly with your ketogenic diet. Each 25g serving provides an impressive 7.5g of fat from medium chain triglycerides (MCTs) extracted from coconut oil, as well as a healthy hit of protein. All this, with no compromise on flavour so you can enjoy a delicious instant coffee with the nutritional profile to suit your diet.Keto Coffee can be consumed as part of a ketogenic diet.

### Purpose

We created our Keto Coffee specifically for individuals following a ketogenic diet. It is great for adding in some extra protein and fat. If you’re already on your keto journey, we recommend consuming 1-3 servings per day.
