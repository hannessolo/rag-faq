### Gut Health Powder

#### Strong gut. Stronger you.

Get your gut in optimal health with our powder packed with powerful ingredients. It blends collagen with fermented whey protein and L-glutamine – a potent trio to strengthen from within³.

Biotin supports mucous membranes¹, calcium fuels digestive enzymes⁴, selenium protects against oxidative stress⁵ and vitamin C supports immunity².

These powerhouse ingredients help you take charge of your gut health. They’re supported by inulin from chicory, baobab, digestive enzymes, and LactoSpore® live cultures.

#### Why should I take it?

**– FUEL DIGESTIVE ENZYMES** with calcium⁴

**– PROTECT AGAINST OXIDATIVE STRESS** with vitamin C and selenium⁵

**– SUPPORT YOUR GUT’S BARRIER** with biotin and vitamin A¹
