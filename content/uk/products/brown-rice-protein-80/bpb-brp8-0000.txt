### Brown Rice Protein

Our super-soluble Brown Rice Protein is one of the best plant-based protein sources. If you’re looking to add more protein to your healthy and balanced lifestyle, this is an ideal choice.

What’s even better? There are so many ways to make it your own. Add to fresh greens in a spinach quiche, bake into vegan biscuits, shake into smoothies or simply add milk or water. It’s unflavoured, so the culinary options are endless.

Brown Rice Protein is a complete protein source with a comprehensive amino acid profile. It has an impressive 80% protein content, packing 24g protein into every serving. It’s also low in fat (1.1g per serving), contains no sugar and just 112 calories per serving.

Did you know? Some amino acids are difficult to obtain on a plant-based diet. As a complete protein source, Brown Rice Protein contains all the essential amino acids your body needs. It’s also one of the most digestible. As far as vegan protein sources go, it doesn’t get much better than this.

Directions

### Directions

Mix 1 scoop (approximately 30g) Brown Rice Protein with 250ml water in a shaker bottle.

We recommend 1-3 servings per day, but in accordance with your own protein requirements.

You could enjoy a shake in the morning, mix into a smoothie or stir into porridge. Suitable any time of day to increase your protein intake, particularly as part of a healthy lifestyle.
