### Sucralose

Our 100% pure sucralose powder, 600 times sweeter than table sugar with zero calories, in a superior form to typical supermarket products. Ideal for sweetening shakes, drinks and recipes without the calorie overload.

Sucralose is a tabletop sweetener that’s used for a wide range of culinary purposes. It’s an ideal sugar substitute for anyone on an energy-restricted diet who wants to cut their calorie intake without sacrificing their sweet tooth.

\\

### Purpose

As a sugar substitute, you can use sucralose for virtually any purpose for which you might normally use sugar. That includes sweetening tea, coffee, shakes and formulas, or even adding to home baking recipes. It’s 600 times sweeter than sugar, so you only need to use a fraction of sugar.
