### Short Resistance Bands

For when you can’t resist another workout.

From strength training to rehabilitation, resistance bands are a must-have for any gym bag or home workout. Made from a premium polyester and elastodiene blend, these **Bulk™** Short Resistance Bands are available as light, medium or heavy.

**Bulk™** Light Resistance Bands provide around 15lbs - 25lbs (7kg-12kg) of resistance and are ideal for warming-up and high repetition ranges. They are offered in a light grey colourway and are contained within a handy drawstring mesh carry bag.

**Bulk™** Medium Resistance Bands provide around 25lbs - 35lbs (12kg-16kg) of resistance and are ideal for a multitude of exercises and repetition ranges. They are offered in a dark grey colourway and are contained within a handy drawstring mesh carry bag.

**Bulk™** Heavy Resistance Bands provide around 40lbs - 50lbs (18kg-23kg) of resistance. This makes them the most challenging bands within our range and they are ideal for when you really want to push yourself to the maximum. They are offered in a black colourway and are contained within a handy drawstring mesh carry bag.
