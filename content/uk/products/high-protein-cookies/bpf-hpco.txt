### High Protein Cookie

Delicious, high protein cookies, delivering the ultimate in flavour, texture and nutrition. Each cookie is packed with 28g of protein, making them perfect for snacking on the go or as an extra source of nutrition between meals.

Our high protein cookies are a soft-baked, high-protein, gelatin-free treat. High in protein and available in two great flavours, they’re a perfect for reaching your daily protein target without unnecessary sugars.
