### Hydration for hardcore workouts

Smashing through an intense training session? You’re sweating out vital minerals. Replenish them with our refreshing Electrolyte Plus and remain ready for your workout.

Directions
----------

### Directions

Flavoured:

Mix 1.5 scoops (approximately 7 g) with 500ml water and shake well. Use 1 serving per day.

Unflavoured:

Mix 2 scoops (approximately 3.1g) with 500ml water and shake well. Use 1 serving per day.

Take it during intense workouts to stay locked in, fresh and ready to smash the session.
