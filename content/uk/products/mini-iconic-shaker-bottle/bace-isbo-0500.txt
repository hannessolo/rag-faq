### Mini Iconic™ Shaker Bottle

Small but perfectly formed.

Make it known to all that you’re part of #teambulk when you rep this **Bulk™** Mini Iconic™ Shaker Bottle.

Dishwasher safe and BPA free, the **Bulk™** Mini Iconic™ Shaker Bottle is suitable for all cold and non-carbonated drinks.
