### 4 Seed Peanut Butter

High quality peanut butter blended with linseeds, chia seeds, pumpkin seeds and sunflower seeds, manufactured exclusively for  **Bulk™.**  Full of flavour and perfect for bulking up with natural nutrition.

Our 4 Seed Peanut Butter is exactly what it sounds like: A rich, natural butter produced from whole roasted peanuts with the unique addition of four different seeds – linseeds, chia seeds, pumpkin seeds and sunflower seeds. It has no added sugar, palm oil or preservatives, and only a small amount of sea salt to enhance the flavour. With 26g of protein and 50g of fat per 100g, it’s an ideal option for any mass gain programme.
