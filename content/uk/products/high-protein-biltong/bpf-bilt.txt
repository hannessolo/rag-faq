### High Protein Biltong

Premium, protein rich biltong, manufactured exclusively for Bulk™ using only the best cuts of silverside beef, paleo friendly, and available in 3 delicious flavours.

Our High Protein Biltong 100g is a delicious and convenient protein-packed snack. Each 100g bag is packed with over 50g of protein, equivalent to 250g of premium silverside beef. Marinated in a blend of herbs and spices, cut into strips, and vacuum packed for maximum freshness, it’s a great way to increase your protein intake on the go.

With over 50g protein in a single pack, our biltong is an ideal way to reach your daily protein target with minimal unnecessary carbs and fat. And best of all, it’s paleo-friendly.
