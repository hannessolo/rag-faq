Capping Kits
============

category

accessories-clothing/capping-kits

**Capping Kits**, also known as capsule filling machines, are a great way to save money on your supplements.

Even though we have a fantastic range of ready-made capsule & tablet supplements, it is impossible to offer all our products in this format. This is where **Capping Kits** come in. They enable you to encapsulate any of our powders, helping you to save money versus buying pre-encapsulated products.

**Capping Kits** are also ideal for some of our powders, particularly those that leave a lot to be desired in the taste department. Once you own a capping kit it will last forever; all you need to do is make sure you have some of our **[Empty Vegetarian Capsules](https://staging.bulk.com/uk/products/vegetarian-capsules/bpb-vcap-0000)** to use it with!
