### **Instructions**

1.) Mix the dry ingredients together in a bowl.

2.) Mash the ripe bananas and add the almond milk, egg whites and 100g of blueberries and combine with the dry ingredients.

3.) Pour into a loaf tin and top with the remaining 50g of blueberries.

4.) Bake at 160° for about 30-35 minutes.
