**WHICH WHEY PROTEIN IS FOR YOU?**
----------------------------------

As illustrated in the table above, Essential Whey Protein is a more cost-effective whey protein concentrate. It’s less advanced than [**Pure Whey Protein™**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/pure-whey-protein/bpb-wpc8-0000), making it higher in carbohydrate and fat, but delivers all the benefits you’d expect from other whey proteins. 

If you’re looking to hit your protein and budget targets with a tasty shake, Essential Whey Protein is a winning choice. But if you’d like a little more protein per serving, and less carbohydrate and fat, we’d recommend our Pure Whey Protein™ and [**Pure Whey Isolate™**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/pure-whey-isolate-90/bpb-wpi9-0000) products. It’s that simple.
