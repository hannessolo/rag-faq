Pure Whey Protein**™**
----------------------

If you’re looking for a pure source of protein to add to your foods, shakes or simply consume after exercise, [Pure Whey Protein™](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/pure-whey-protein/bpb-wpc8-0000) is the one. It’s available in over 15 great flavours and provides 24g of protein per serving.

Informed Whey Protein
---------------------

[**Informed Whey Protein,**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/informed-whey/bpps-iwhe) as the name suggests, is part of the [**Bulk™ Pro**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/shop-by-range/pro-series.html) range. Just like the other products in our Pro range, Informed Whey was manufactured by our expert team to be the best protein powder available, regardless of expense. Because of this, it has some additional active ingredients that Pure Whey Protein**™** does not:

*   Leucine and Glutamine which are essential amino acids that are important after intense exercise
*   Probiotics in the form of Lactospore®
*   Digestive enzymes in the form of DigeZyme®
*   The unique mTOR Whey Complex offers guaranteed 81% protein and boasts an excellent amino acid profile

The short answer
----------------

So to summarise, if you’re looking for a pure source of protein to add to your foods, shakes or simply consume after exercise, **Pure Whey Protein™** is the one. It’s a no-nonsense, high quality, tasty protein powder. We also have **Essential Whey Protein** if you want an even more affordable alternative.

If you don’t care about cost, Informed Whey offers professional, batch-tested quality: the best protein powder money can buy. It’s more expensive, but if you’re really serious about training, then it’s a great option,
