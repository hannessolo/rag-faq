Recipe: No-bake protein brownies
================================

[Recipes](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/diet-and-nutrition/recipes/)

![](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/wp-content/uploads/2022/12/No-bake-protein-brownies-1-1012x674.jpg)

Hungry for a chocolate fix? These protein brownies are deliciously chocolatey while satisfying a tasty macro hit. Try them now with our recipe below. 

**What are the macros and calories?**
-------------------------------------

*   273 Kcals
*   24g Protein
*   14g Fat
*   16g Carbs
*   7g Fibre

This protein brownies recipe serves 8.

**What ingredients do you need?**
---------------------------------

*   150g [**Almond Butter**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/roasted-almond-butter/bpf-abut)
*   5 tbsp Golden Syrup [**Zero Calorie Syrup™**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/zero-calorie-syrup.html)
*   7 tbsp [**Oat Drink**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/oat-drink.html)
*   100g Chocolate [**Gourmet Vegan Protein**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/gourmet-vegan-protein.html) or [**Pure Whey Protein™**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/pure-whey-protein.html)
*   25g [**Organic Cacao Powder**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/organic-cacao-powder.html)

**How do you make it?**
-----------------------

*   Mix all the ingredients into a smooth dough.
*   Press into a lined loaf tin and freeze for 1 hour. 
*   Slice into 8 squares, then dip in the melted chocolate. 
*   Refrigerate until the chocolate is set and enjoy!
