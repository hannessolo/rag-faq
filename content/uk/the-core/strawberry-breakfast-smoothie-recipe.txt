### Summary

Start the day as you mean to go on; like a machine. This Strawberry Breakfast Smoothie recipe delivers an impressive 16.7g of protein and 20g of slow-release carbs per serving and it takes all of 30 seconds to make.

### Instructions

1.) Blitz it all up in a blender.

2.) Pour into a fancy cup and enjoy.

**Makes 3 servings, which you can store in fridge and keep it as your on the go breakfast!**
