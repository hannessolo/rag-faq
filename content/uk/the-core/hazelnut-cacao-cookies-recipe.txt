Hazelnut & Cacao Cookies Recipe
===============================

[08th September 201630th August 2022](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/hazelnut-cacao-cookies-recipe/)

[Recipes](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/diet-and-nutrition/recipes/)

![Hazelnut & Cacao Cookies](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/hazelnut-cacao-cookies-recipe.jpg)

What happens when you mix some of our amazing **Hazelnut Butter** with **Organic Cacao Liquor Button**s? You guessed it – you get this incredible **Hazelnut & Cacao Cookies recipe** absolutely bursting with nutritious glory! And at less than 90 calories per cookie, it’s worth helping yourself to a few!

**Instructions:**

1.) Preheat oven to 180 Degrees C and line a baking tray with greaseproof baking paper.

2.) Add hazelnut butter to a bowl, and in order listed add the remaining ingredients combining well between each addition.

3.) Roll into 9 balls, place onto lined baking tray and press down with fingers so that you end up with chunky/ flat rounds.

4.) Bake for approximately 10 minutes until golden around edges.

5.) Leave to cool completely or place in fridge so that it cools quicker.

Recipe yields 10 cookies.
