By this point, you already know why we love smoothies so much. They’re a convenient way to get all the nutrients you need in one quick and easy recipe. Not only this vegan muscle builder smoothie tastes amazing but our [Vegan Protein Powder](https://www.bulk.com/uk/vegan-protein-powder.html) provides each shake with over 23g protein per serving from 5 premium plant-based sources. It’s the ideal choice to increase your protein intake. Throw in a banana, almond butter and some honey for the most indulgent recipe that will help you build muscle and achieve your goals. Building muscle never tasted so good.

Ingredients:

*   1 scoop of [Vegan Protein Powder](https://www.bulk.com/uk/vegan-protein-powder.html) in Caramel Latte
*   1 frozen banana
*   1 tbsp of [Almond Butter](https://www.bulk.com/uk/roasted-almond-butter.html)
*   2 dates
*   A squeeze of [Zero Calorie Syrup](https://www.bulk.com/uk/zero-calorie-syrup.html) in Honey

Method:

*   Mix
*   Blend
*   Drizzle with Zero Calorie Syrup and enjoy!

Macros:

*   376 kcals
*   26g protein
*   48g carbs
