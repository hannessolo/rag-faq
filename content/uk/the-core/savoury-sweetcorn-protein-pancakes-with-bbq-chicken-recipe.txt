### Instructions:

1.) Add the dry ingredients to a bowl and mix to combine.

2.) Add the wet ingredients along with the sweetcorn and mix to form a stiff batter (you may need to add more almond milk if the batter seems too thick).

3.) Put a frying pan on low heat, spray it with oil and fry spoonfuls  of the pancake batter, flipping them once.

4.) Put a seperate pan on high heat and spray with oil.

5.) Coat the chicken filet in BBQ seasoning and fry for about 5 minutes or until cooked, turning once.

6.) Top the pancakes with the chicken and serve with BBQ sauce and salad.
