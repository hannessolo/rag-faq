Banana & Peanut Butter Muffins Recipe
=====================================

[Recipes](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/diet-and-nutrition/recipes/)

Instructions
------------

1.) Preheat oven to 160 degrees C.

2.) Mash bananas in a bowl and fold in remaining ingredients until all are well combined.

3.) Pour into a muffin mould or lined muffin tray.

4.) Bake for 15-20 mins or until baked through and golden.

Recipe yields 12 muffins.

**Tip**: for a lower calorie option, combine [Zero Calorie Syrup™](https://www.bulk.com/uk/zero-calorie-syrup.html) with agave by using half and half sub.

Top with more peanut butter and the new Vegan Protein Bar Banana Flavour for the ultimate treat!
