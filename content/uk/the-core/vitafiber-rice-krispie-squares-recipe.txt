### Method:

1.) Add the **VitaFiber™ powder** in a small pan on a low heat.

2.) Add the 30 mls water and start to whisk the mixture to combine.

3.) Keep whisking until to mixture turns from cloud to a clear syrup.

4.) Add the **whey protein** and puffed rice to a separate bowl.

5.) Pour the syrup into the **whey protein** quickly and carefully fold to combine.

6.) Shape the dough into a square baking tray.

7.) Bake at 170ºC for about 10 minutes.

**To make the topping:  
**  
1.) Add the dry ingredients together in a mug, add the almond milk and mix.

2.) Allow it to cool before slicing into squares and add the chocolate topping.
