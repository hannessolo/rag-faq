Valentines Protein Peanut Butter Hearts Recipe
==============================================

[Recipes](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/diet-and-nutrition/recipes/)

![Protein Peanut Butter Hearts](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/IMG_4772edit.jpg)

Love is… Protein, Chocolate and Nut Butter – what better combination! This Protein Peanut Butter Hearts Recipe is quick and easy and sure to impress more than your average box of chocolates this Valentine’s Day.

**INSTRUCTIONS**:

1.  Melt the chocolate and coconut oil on a low heat until combined.
2.  Spoon enough of the mixture to cover the bottom of each heart silicone mould.
3.  Place in the freezer for 3-5 minutes to slightly set.
4.  Once the chocolate has set, put ½ tsp of Nuts About Whey™ peanut butter into the centre of each mould and gently flatten with the back of a spoon.
5.  Scoop more of the melted chocolate mixture into the mould to cover the peanut butter.
6.  Return to the freezer for approximately 15 minutes before serving.
