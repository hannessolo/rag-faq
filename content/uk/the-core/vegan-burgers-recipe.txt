Vegan Burgers Recipe
====================

[15th October 201926th November 2024](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/vegan-burgers-recipe/)

[Recipes](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/diet-and-nutrition/recipes/)

![](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/Anna-Carolina-Freiheit-1-BLOG-SOCIAL-674x674.jpeg)

Proving burgers don’t have to clog the arteries. This amazing vegan burger recipe is from #TeamBulk ambassador [@healthyliciousvegan](http://www.instagram.com/healthyliciousvegan), and whether you’re vegan or just keen to cut down on the meat you eat, this one is for you.

**Ingredients (makes 10 burgers)**

*   500g cooked kidney beans
*   150g cooked Quinoa
*   1 red onion
*   2 cloves of garlic
*   1 beetroot
*   4 tbsp [Instant Oats](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/gluten-free-ultra-fine-oats/bpb-oats-gfre)
*   1 tsp Garlic & Herb Seasoning
*   1 tsp Smoke & Spice Seasoning
*   Salt and pepper, to taste
*   [Coconut Oil](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/organic-virgin-coconut-oil/bpf-ovco) for frying

**Instructions**

*   Blend the beans, beetroot, onion and garlic in a food processor
*   Add the mixture to a bowl and add all of the other ingredients, mixing well with your hands
*   Shape into 10 burger patties
*   Fry with some coconut oil until crispy
*   Build your burger with your preferred toppings, and enjoy!
