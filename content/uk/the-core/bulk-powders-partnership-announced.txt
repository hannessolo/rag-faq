BULK POWDERS™ are now the official nutrition supplier to Matt Argall
--------------------------------------------------------------------

We’re delighted to announce that BULK POWDERS™ are now the official nutrition supplier to Matt Argall, the 2 x NPA British Lightweight Champion.

Last week, Matt won the Natural Physique Association (NPA) British Lightweight title for the 2nd consecutive year. Matt has a conditioned, aesthetic physique and looked sensational in winning his class. The NPA is a drug free for life federation that tests through urinalysis and polygraph testing.

Importantly for BULK POWDERS™, Matt genuinely uses our products and has provided great feedback. Pre-contest, Matt incorporates [Complete All in One](https://www.bulk.com/uk/complete-all-in-one.html "BULK POWDERS All In One Recovery Protein")™, [Instant BCAA](https://www.bulk.com/uk/instantised-bcaa.html "BULK POWDERS Instant BCAA") and [HMB](https://www.bulk.com/uk/hmb-powder-2.html "BULK POWDERS HMB Powder") into his meticulous nutrition plan.

We’re really pleased to welcome Matt to the BULK POWDERS™ team and look forward to supporting him in any way we can. Matt really knows his stuff and we’re equally looking forward to sharing blogs and updates from Matt.
