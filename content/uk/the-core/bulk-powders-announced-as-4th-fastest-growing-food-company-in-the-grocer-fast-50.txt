BULK POWDERS® Announced As 4th Fastest Growing Food Company In The Grocer Fast 50
=================================================================================

[10th November 201719th August 2022](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/bulk-powders-announced-as-4th-fastest-growing-food-company-in-the-grocer-fast-50/)

[Bulk™ News](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/news-and-updates/bulk-news-and-updates/)

![BULK POWDERS - Grocer Fast 50](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/grocer-fast-50.jpg)

We’re extremely pleased to announce that after delivering another fantastic year of growth we’ve placed 4th in The Grocer’s 2017 Fast 50 – the fastest growing private food companies in the UK. Privately owned food & drink companies are growing faster than ever and BULK POWDERS® is very much one of the rising stars.

The 2017 Grocer Fast 50 was more competitive than ever this year with a record 33 new entries. Suppliers have needed a CAGR (compound annual growth rate) of 36.2% to make it into the top 10, compared with 27.8% in 2016 which showcases how the market has evolved.

BULK POWDERS® was started by Adam Rossiter & Elliot Dawes in 2006 and has grown rapidly to be the No 2 Sports Nutrition brand in the UK (Revenue approaching £35m in 2017 & expecting to achieve in excess of £50m in 2018) with strong growth across Europe in 13 different countries. 2017 will see an industry leading 50 products & ranges launched. These are exciting times and there is a lot more to come.

BULK POWDERS® offers people the highest quality [Sports Nutrition](https://www.bulk.com/uk/sports-nutrition.html) to put you on track for peak performance. Whether you’re a casual gym-goer or a seasoned pro, BULK POWDERS® will help you to achieve your personal best every day. With four key ranges, encapsulating the Pro, Pure & Complete Series, as well as [Active Foods](https://www.bulk.com/uk/shop-by-range/active-foods.html), BULK POWDERS® has it all and simply will not be beaten on price, quality or service.

BULK POWDERS® is not only expanding its range of products in the near future but very much focused on further driving brand awareness, equity & sales. This has included signing well known athletes to represent the brand including World Champion & Olympic diver Tom Daley.

Elliot Dawes, Managing Director BULK POWDERS® comments: “It’s a fantastic achievement to be ranked 4th in The Grocer Fast 50. It showcases how far we have come as a business. We have a really hard working & talented team behind us and it’s very much paying off.”
