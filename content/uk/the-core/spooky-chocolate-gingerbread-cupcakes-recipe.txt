### Summary

Sure to be a favourite with the kids and adults alike! These Chocolate Gingerbread Cupcakes look the business and taste amazing too. How cool is the icing?!

### Instructions

1.) Preheat the oven to 160C.  
2.) In a bowl, combine the dry ingredients. Add the wet ingredients and mix/blend until smooth.  
3.) Divide between 6 large silicone cake cases and bake for 22-25 minutes.

**Icing:**

1.) Mix greek yoghurt and truvia to taste.

**Web Effect:**

1.) Mix 1 tbsp cocoa powder, 1 tsp truvia & 1 tbsp water to a paste.  
2.) Microwave for a few seconds, stir and leave to cool.  
3.) Pipe concentric circles on top of the greek yoghurt and drag a cocktail stick through the circles.
