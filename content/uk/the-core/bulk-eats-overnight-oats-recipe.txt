Overnight oats – this small but mighty breakfast option is the perfect grab-and-go meal for an active lifestyle. Prep it the night before, thank yourself in the morning. All you need to do is follow the super-easy steps from our overnight oats recipe video below.

For a twist on a classic, combine our [Pure Whey Protein](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/pure-whey-protein/bpb-wpc8-0000) with milk and oats and leave in the fridge overnight. Wake up to a delicious, thick and creamy breakfast designed to keep you fuller for longer and help smash your fitness goals. Take it one step further and spice up this simple recipe by experimenting with different toppings to tailor to your taste.

Ingredients:

*   50g oats
*   100ml milk (or milk alternative)
*   One scoop of [Pure Whey Protein](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/pure-whey-protein/bpb-wpc8-0000)
*   [Dried Goji Berries](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/dried-goji-berries/bpf-goji) for topping

Method:

*   Mix oats, Pure Whey Protein and milk
*   Leave in the fridge overnight
*   Top as desired
*   Enjoy!

Macros:

*   409 kcal
*   36g protein
*   43g carbs
*   8g fat
