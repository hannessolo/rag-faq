National Smoothie Day may be over, but smoothies are here to stay.

Featuring our Clear Whey All In One, this Protein Passionfruit Smoothie contributes towards the growth and maintenance of muscle mass and offers a complete post-workout formula. Make gains, drink smoothies. Life is good. 

This smoothie was featured in our picks for [**5 Easy High-Protein Smoothie Recipes**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/5-easy-high-protein-smoothie-recipes/).

**What are the macros and calories?**
-------------------------------------

*   376 Kcals
*   33g Protein
*   52g Carbs
*   5g Fat

**What ingredients do you need?**
---------------------------------

*   100g frozen mango
*   2 scoops [**Passionfruit Clear Whey All In One**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/clear-all-in-one/bble-cwa1)
*   (or [Clear Whey Isolate)](https://www.bulk.com/uk/clear-whey-isolate.html)
*   1 tbsp milled flaxseed
*   200ml-300ml [**Oat Drink**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/oat-drink.html) **o**r Milk
*   Passionfruit to serve
