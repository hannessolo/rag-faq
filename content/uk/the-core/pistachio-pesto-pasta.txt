Pistachio Pesto Pasta
=====================

Pistachio Pesto Pasta is perfect for a quick and easy mid-week dinner or meal-prep. The recipe uses homemade basil pesto, which has a delicious fresh and herby flavour. Of course if you are short on time, you can use any pre-made pesto. With our delicious, natural butter made from 100% roasted whole pistachios and nothing else, this one is sure to make it into your weekly dinner rotation.

### Ingredients (serves 4):

*   200g [Pistachio Butter⁣](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/pistachio-butter/bpf-pisb)
*   50ml olive oil⁣
*   1 clove of garlic⁣
*   10 fresh basil leaves⁣
*   2tsp grated parmesan ⁣
*   Salt & pepper to taste⁣
*   Pasta of your choice⁣

### Method:

1.  Place your pasta on to boil according to package instructions⁣
2.  Blend all other ingredients in a food processor until finely chopped and season with salt & pepper⁣
3.  Once boiled, add 2-3 tbsp pasta water into the sauce⁣
4.  Mix into the cooked pasta⁣
5.  And your Pistachio Pesto Pasta is ready to serve! ⁣
