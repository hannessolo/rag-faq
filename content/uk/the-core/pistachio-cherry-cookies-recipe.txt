### Instructions

1.) Pre-heat the oven to 180 degrees C.

2.) In a bowl, mix together the dry ingredients, except the dried cherries.

3.) Add in the wet and mix to form a batter.

4.) Stir through the dried cherries so that they are evenly distributed throughout the batter.

5.) Drop equal amounts of the batter into cookie shapes onto a silicone or baking paper lined tray and bake for 15 minutes.

6.) Leave to cool.
