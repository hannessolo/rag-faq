### Instructions:

1.) Preheat the oven to 180C

2.) Mix all the ingredients together in a bowl, or add to a small food processor and blitz until combined into a thick dough.

3.) Divide the mix into 6 balls, flatten with your hands and place on a lined baking tray. Chill in the fridge for 10 minutes. Bake for 10 minutes. Allow to fully cool before eating.
