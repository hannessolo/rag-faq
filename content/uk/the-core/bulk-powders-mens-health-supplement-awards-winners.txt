How the awards were decided
---------------------------

**Examination** – Independent supplement analysts trawled through research papers to determine which chemicals actually work and which are just hype

**Distillation** – Men’s Health nutritionists looked over the findings and created their final shortlist for each award category. The nutritionists were asked to consider potency of compounds and a lack of unnecessary and unpleasant additives

**Application** – The nutritionists then chose winners whilst also considering bio-availability of the supplement and its value for money
