Recipe: High-Protein Cheesecake (Strawberry Flavour)
====================================================

[05th September 202225th November 2024](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/recipe-high-protein-cheesecake-strawberry-flavour/)

[Recipes](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/diet-and-nutrition/recipes/)

![high-protein cheesecake recipe](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/2-1012x674.jpg)

Cheesecake is a dessert hall-of-fame. But often high-calorie, high in sugar, cheesecakes are difficult to justify in your diet. That’s where we come in. With this special high-protein cheesecake recipe, we show you how to make your favourite dessert with just 137 calories.

**What are the macros and calories?**
-------------------------------------

*   137 Kcals
*   13g Protein
*   6g Fat
*   8g Carbs

**What ingredients do you need?**
---------------------------------

### **For the base**

*   2x Speculoos MACRO MUNCH™ [**Protein Biscuits**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/macro-munch-protein-biscuits/mmun-bisc)
*   ½ tbsp [**Organic Virgin Coconut Oil**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/organic-virgin-coconut-oil.html), melted

### **For the cheesecake**

*   6g gelatine powder
*   60ml hot water
*   140g light cream cheese
*   60g fat free Greek yoghurt
*   2 scoops Strawberry [**Pure Whey Protein™**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/pure-whey-protein.html)

### **For the topping**

*   150g chopped strawberries
*   2-3 tbsp water
*   Optional – [**LiquiFlav™**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/liquiflav/bp-liqu-50ml)

**How do you make it?**
-----------------------

*   Blend the biscuits and coconut oil into crumbs. Press into the base of a silicone muffin tray or cupcake liners.
*   Mix the hot water and gelatine powder until completely dissolved. 
*   Blend together the cheesecake ingredients, then pour onto the bases. Refrigerate for 4-5 hours.
*   Cook the strawberries and water on a medium heat for 5-7 minutes. Leave to cool completely.
*   Top each cheesecake with the cooled strawberry sauce and enjoy.
