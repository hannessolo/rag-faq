Vegan Protein Cookie Dough Overnight Oats Recipe
================================================

[Recipes](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/diet-and-nutrition/recipes/)

![](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/wp-content/uploads/2022/05/blog-image-vegan-overnight-oats-1012x674.jpg)

Looking to add green(er) dishes into your day? This plant-based breakfast bowl is just for you. With the help of our Vanilla Vegan Protein Powder and Flavoured Peanut Butter in Cookies & Cream (an absolute winner), we’re taking overnight oats to a whole new level. At 23g protein per serving, you’re off to a healthy and satiating start.

Ingredients
-----------

*   50g (1/2 cup) oats
*   2tbsp Bulk™ [Vanilla Vegan Protein Powder](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/vegan-protein-powder/vser-vppo)
*   120ml (1/2 cup) Oat Drink
*   4 tbsp (1/4 cup) dairy-free yoghurt
*   1 tbsp Bulk™ [Flavoured Peanut Butter – Cookies & Cream](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/flavoured-peanut-butters/bpf-pbut-flav)
*   1/2 Bulk™ [Vegan Protein Brownie](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/vegan-protein-brownie/vser-vpbr), cut into small squares

Method
------

Mix together your oats, Vanilla Vegan Protein Powder, Bulk™ Oat Drink, dairy-free yoghurt and Bulk™ Flavoured Peanut Butter then cover and allow to chill overnight in the fridge.

When ready to serve, fold in the chopped protein brownie and top with more peanut butter.
