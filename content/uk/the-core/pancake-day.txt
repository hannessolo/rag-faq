**3\. Banana protein pancake recipe**
-------------------------------------

This protein pancake batter keeps it simple. With all the usual ingredients combined and two scoops of [**whey protein**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/protein/whey-protein.html), it leaves room for you to play around with toppings. The perfect way to enjoy a delicious treat while still hitting your nutrition goals.

![bulk-protein-pancakes](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/wp-content/uploads/2020/02/protein-pancakes-banner1-533x300.jpg)

### **Ingredients**

*   One banana
*   2 scoops of [**protein powder**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/protein)
*   2 eggs
*   1/2 tsp of baking powder
*   1/2 a cup of oat milk
*   A dash of cinnamon

### **Method**

*   Blend your ingredients together
*   Pour carefully into a non-stick pan to form small circles
*   Flip once small bubbles appear on top
*   Stack, top and enjoy.

### **Macros and calories**

*   286 kcal
*   31g protein
*   25g carbs
*   9g fat

**5\. Biscoff protein pancakes**
--------------------------------

[Joe Delaney](https://www.instagram.com/shredbundy/) is an expert protein pancake maker, so we asked him to whip up another delicious recipe for us. Incorporating the ever-popular Biscoff biscuit spread, this is another winner.

[https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Bulk-pancake-reel.mp4](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Bulk-pancake-reel.mp4)

### **Ingredients**

*   75g oats
*   30g protein powderÂ 
*   60ml egg whites
*   100g fat-free yoghurt
*   1/2 tsp bicarbonate of soda
*   1 dollop of [**peanut butter**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/peanut-butter-1kg/bpf-pbut)
*   Biscoff spread
*   1 banana

### **Method:**

*   Mix all pancake batter ingredients into a blender in this order: egg whites, oats, peanut butter, yoghurt, protein powder and bicarbonate of soda.
*   Blend them up.
*   Fry a ladle of the pancake batter over medium-high heat for around 30 seconds on each side.
*   Repeat with the remaining batter.
*   Cut up a banana into slices and melt some Biscoff (in a microwave or a frying pan).
*   Pile up the pancakes, then top with banana and drizzle over the melted Biscoff.
