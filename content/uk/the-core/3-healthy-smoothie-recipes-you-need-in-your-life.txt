Smoothies are a quick and easy way to refuel
--------------------------------------------

Love them or loathe them, smoothies are a quick and easy way to refuel, but it’s important to top up your body’s engine with the right nutrition. Here’s a list of 3 healthy smoothie recipes that can be prepared and consumed in less than 5 minutes, including an option for breakfast, a flexible ‘anytime’ option and a post-workout giant.

### Recipe #1 – Strawberry Breakfast Smoothie

Start the day as you mean to go on; like a machine. This Strawberry Breakfast Smoothie recipe delivers an impressive 16.7g of protein and 20g of slow-release carbs per serving and it takes all of 30 seconds to make.

**MACROS:** 177 Kcals | 16.7g Protein | 20g Carbs | 3.1g Fats

\>> [Blending instructions for the Strawberry Breakfast Smoothie recipe](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/strawberry-breakfast-smoothie-recipe/ "Strawberry Breakfast Smoothie Recipe")

### Recipe #2 – Green Maca Smoothie

A quick and easy Green [Maca](../../maca-powder.html "Maca Powder") Smoothie recipe to help you take on the world! Add a scoop of [Pure Whey Protein™](../../pure-whey-protein.html "Pure Whey Protein") (Peaches & Cream) or unflavoured whey to increase the protein content.

**MACROS:** 88 Kcals | 1.5g Protein | 21.1g Carbs | 0.1g Fat

\>>[Blending instructions for the Green Maca Smoothie recipe](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/green-maca-smoothie-recipe/ "Green Maca Smoothie Recipe")

### Recipe #3 – Choconana

Check out this powerful post-workout [Choconana Smoothie recipe](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/choconana-smoothie-recipe/ "Choconana Smoothie Recipe"). It’s packed full of carbs to help replenish your energy levels after a hard session, with a good amount of protein and fats too. There’s even some creatine in there to help you recover strong!

**MACROS:** 223 Kcals | 9.3g Protein | 28.6g Carbs | 9g Fats
