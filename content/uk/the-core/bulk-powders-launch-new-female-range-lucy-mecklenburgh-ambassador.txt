BULK POWDERS® Launch New Female Range with Lucy Mecklenburgh As Ambassador
==========================================================================

[03rd January 201825th August 2022](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/bulk-powders-launch-new-female-range-lucy-mecklenburgh-ambassador/)

[Bulk™ News](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/news-and-updates/bulk-news-and-updates/)

BULK POWDERS® has announced the launch of their new female range, [WMN®](https://www.bulk.com/uk/shop-by-range/wmn.html), formulated specifically for women to support a healthy & active lifestyle.

The WMN® range is a selection of bespoke active nutrition products which encapsulates the everyday needs of busy women. From [Daily Protein™](https://www.bulk.com/uk/women-daily-protein-shake.html), [Lean Shake™](https://www.bulk.com/uk/women-lean-shake-weight-loss-protein-shake.html) & [Lean 11™](https://www.bulk.com/uk/lean-11.html) for toning to [Radiance Capsules™](https://www.bulk.com/uk/radiance-capsules-selenium-biotin.html) & [Active WMN™](https://www.bulk.com/uk/active-wmn-women-multivitamins.html) for vitality, the range is designed to support all aspects of women’s health and fitness.

WMN® joins [PURE SERIES®](https://www.bulk.com/uk/shop-by-range/pure-series.html), [PRO SERIES®](https://www.bulk.com/uk/shop-by-range/pro-series.html), [COMPLETE SERIES®](https://www.bulk.com/uk/shop-by-range/complete-series.html) and [ACTIVE FOODS®](https://www.bulk.com/uk/shop-by-range/active-foods.html) as the latest addition to the BULK POWDERS® ranges.

Also joining the team, as the face of the new WMN® range, is TV star & entrepreneur **Lucy Mecklenburgh**. As a fitness fanatic & health-conscious foodie, Lucy is passionate about maintaining a healthy diet & lifestyle that will keep her in great shape, making her the perfect partner for the range.

BULK POWDERS® offers the highest quality sports nutrition to help you along the way. Whether you’re a casual gym-goer or a seasoned pro, BULK POWDERS® will help you to achieve your personal best every day.

**Of the partnership, Lucy Mecklenburgh** **said:** “I’m really pleased to be working with BULK POWDERS®. The new WMN® range is formulated for women & addresses a key gap in the market. I’m always on-the-go so it’s amazing to know I can rely on WMN® to incorporate the added protein & vitamins I need into my diet, quickly and easily. WMN® really has all you need.”

With five key ranges, now including the WMN® collection, BULK POWDERS® has it all and simply will not be beaten on price, quality or service – and now has the Lucy Mecklenburgh seal of approval.

**Adam Rossiter, Managing Director BULK POWDERS® said**: “This is an incredibly exciting moment for BULK POWDERS® to launch WMN® and we are very pleased to have Lucy on board as the face of the brand. We’re extremely proud of the new WMN® range; it retains our commitment to market leading formulations and quality whilst making our brand more appealing & approachable to a big new demographic. We can’t wait to see what can be achieved together.”

Lucy Mecklenburgh joins [Anthony Joshua](https://www.bulk.com/uk/anthony-joshua), [Tom Daley](https://www.bulk.com/uk/tom-daley), [Jonathan Joseph](https://www.bulk.com/uk/jonathan-joseph) and [Lucy Watson](https://www.bulk.com/uk/lucy-watson) as the latest BULK POWDERS® Ambassador.
