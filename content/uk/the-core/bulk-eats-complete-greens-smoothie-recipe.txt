Don’t just eat your greens – drink them! All you need to do is follow the super-simple steps from our complete greens smoothie recipe video below.

Smoothies are a great quick and easy option for getting the nutrients you need at any time of the day. This gloriously green recipe features our [Complete Greens powder](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/complete-greens/bble-cgre) which is high in protein, high in fibre and boasts 10 super green ingredients making it perfect for those who are looking to add some quality green foods to their diet, without the chewing!

Chuck in a handful of your favourite frozen fruits, berries or something a little bit more tropical, blend and enjoy!

Ingredients:

*   3 scoops of [Complete Greens](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/complete-greens/bble-cgre)
*   250ml of almond milk
*   1 cup of frozen fruit
*   1 spoonful of [Chia Seeds](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/chia-seeds/bpf-chia)

Method:

*   Mix
*   Blend
*   Enjoy!

Macros:

*   208 kcals
*   14.1g protein
*   22.5g carbs
*   6g fat
