BULK POWDERS® Selected In 2018 European Business Awards “Ones To Watch” List
============================================================================

[15th March 201830th August 2022](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/bulk-powders-selected-2018-european-business-awards-ones-watch-list/)

[Bulk™ News](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/news-and-updates/bulk-news-and-updates/)

![European Business Awards](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/European-Business-Awards.jpg)

BULK POWDERS® has been selected as one of just under 200 Companies that will appear on the 2018 European Business Awards ‘Ones to Watch’ list for the UK.

As part of the criteria to make it on the list, the European Business Awards panel look for exceptional growth, significant innovation & an ethical approach to business.  Additionally businesses are only selected if they are fitting one of the 12 Awards categories of the European Business Awards.

Other Companies also selected in the list include Brewdog, Charles Tyrwhitt, Just Eat & Pure Gym.

**Adam Rossiter, Managing Director BULK POWDERS®, comments**: “We are proud to be announced as one of the 2018 European Business Awards ‘Ones to Watch’ & it shows how we are evolving successfully as a business not only in the UK but across Europe. We are looking forward to another successful year in 2018”.

Please see below for more information on the European Business Awards 2018 [https://www.businessawardseurope.com/](https://www.businessawardseurope.com/)
