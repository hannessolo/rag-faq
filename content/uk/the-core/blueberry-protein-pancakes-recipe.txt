**INGREDIENTS FOR BLUEBERRY PROTEIN PANCAKES**
----------------------------------------------

*   4 scoops Blueberry [Protein Pancake Mix](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/protein-pancake-mix/bble-chpp)
*   200ml water/ milk
*   100g blueberries
*   1 tbsp coconut oil, for frying
*   Maple [Zero Calorie Syrup™](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/zero-calorie-syrup/bpf-zcsy)

**METHOD FOR BLUEBERRY PROTEIN PANCAKES**
-----------------------------------------

1.  Mix the protein pancake mix and water in a bowl. Fold in the blueberries.
2.  Heat a little oil in a pan. Fry a large spoonful of the mixture on medium heat for 1-2 minutes each side, until lightly browned. Repeat with the remaining mixture.
3.  Serve with a drizzle of syrup.

**HOW MANY PEOPLE DOES THIS RECIPE SERVE?**
-------------------------------------------

These delicious pancakes serve two people. But if you need more, just double the recipe list.
