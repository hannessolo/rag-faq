With temperatures rising there may be nothing better to cool off with than a refreshing scoop of sorbet. We have found the perfect way to enjoy this sweet treat that is macro-friendly! Here is our Mango and Passionfruit Sorbet, featuring our brand new Clear Whey Isolate, packed with 20g of protein per serving – a perfect post-workout snack.

### **Ingredients (serves 2):**

*   25g [Clear Whey Isolate](https://www.bulk.com/uk/clear-whey-isolate.html) 
*   250g frozen mango
*   Juice of 1 lime
*   2 tbsp [bulk™ Oat Drink](https://www.bulk.com/uk/oat-drink.html)

### **Method:**

Add all the ingredients into a blender and blend until smooth  
You can serve it straight away, or transfer into a baking tin and freeze for an hour for a more solid texture  
Enjoy.
