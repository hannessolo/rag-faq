### Summary

Here’s a supremely easy recipe for some Oat Protein Muffins using our festive Mince Pie flavour Pure Whey Protein™. You quite literally throw everything in a bowl. Mix. Pour. Whack in the oven. Job done!

### Instructions

1.) Mix all of the ingredients together in a large bowl.

2.) Pour the mixture, in equal quantities, into a muffin tray (12)

3.) Bake for 10mins @ 180 (fan assisted) or 200 (standard).
