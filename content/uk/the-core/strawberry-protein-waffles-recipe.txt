Looking for a recipe that will boost your protein intake for the day? The Muscle Bakery has served up these strawberry protein waffles with over 45g protein per 2 waffle serving. Prepare using a waffle iron and indulge in the tasty gains.

### Method:

1\. Add all your ingredients into a blender and blitz until you have a thick but smooth batter-like consistency.  
2\. Heat waffle machine as per instructions, using spray prior to heating.  
3\. Once very hot, pour batter and bake accordingly.

Recipe yields 4 large waffles.
