Coconut Fig Tart Recipe
=======================

[05th January 201630th August 2022](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/coconut-fig-tart-recipe/)

[Recipes](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/diet-and-nutrition/recipes/)

![Coconut Fig Tart Recipe](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/coconut-fig-tart-recipe.jpg)

This is a fantastic high protein treat, especially for those who are partial to a tasty dessert. With 10g protein per slice, and less than 6g carbs, it is the perfect recipe to satisfy your sweet tooth!

Incorporating our **Pure Whey Protein**™, **Complete Protein Dessert**™, **Dried Coconut** and popular **Liquid Egg Whites**, this Coconut Fig Tart recipe is definitely worth a try!

### Instructions:

1.) Mash up your ingredients until you have a smooth dough.

2.) Make sure to save some coconut flakes and a couple of figs to decorate your tart at the end.

3.) Add the dough to a baking form or dish and bake for about 30-40 minutes on medium heat.

4.) After half of the baking time, put the leftover **Dried Coconut** flakes and some fig as a topping on the tart.

5.) After a total 30-40 minutes of baking time, take the tart out of the oven and allow to cool for up to 20 minutes. Enjoy!
