### Instructions

1.) Blend the dry ingredients  
2.) Separate the egg whites and beat with an electric mixer until stiff  
3.) Gently fold the egg whites into the mixture  
4.) Bake for 30-40 minutes at 180°C  
5.) Allow to cool, cover with quark and a final dusting of cinnamon
