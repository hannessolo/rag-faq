### Instructions

1.) Melt the coconut oil and allow to cool slightly

2.) Add the remaining ingredients and mix until evenly distributed

3.) Using a teaspoon and your fingers, press the mixture into the mould and freeze for an hour or so.

4.) Store in a freezer and remember to remove five minutes before eating to soften slightly.

Enjoy!
