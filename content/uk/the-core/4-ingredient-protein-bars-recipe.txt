4-Ingredient Protein Bars Recipe
================================

![4 ingredient protein bars](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/wp-content/uploads/2024/02/Artboard-5-1012x674.jpg)

Enjoy these delicious [protein bars](https://www.bulk.com/uk/protein/protein-bars.html), made with just four ingredients. If you fancy a break from the usual bars, making your own is great fun and is actually more cost-effective. See below for the ingredients, method and nutritional breakdown.

[https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/wp-content/uploads/2024/02/4-ingredient-protein-bars.mp4](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/wp-content/uploads/2024/02/4-ingredient-protein-bars.mp4)

**MACROS AND CALORIES** 
------------------------

**Nutritional Breakdown (estimated per bar):  
**Calories: 269 kcals  
Protein: 18g  
Fat: 19g  
Carbs: 7g  
Fibre: 3g

![](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/wp-content/uploads/2024/02/Artboard-3-2-533x355.jpg)

**INGREDIENTS**
---------------

*   345g Smooth [Peanut Butter](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/peanut-butter-1kg/bpf-pbut)
*   80g [Salted Caramel Pure Whey Protein™](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/pure-whey-protein/bpb-wpc8-0000)
*   Chocolate
*   1-2 tbsp milk (or oat milk)
*   4 tbsp Maple Syrup [Zero Calorie Syrup™](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/zero-calorie-syrup/bpf-zcsy)

**METHOD**
----------

1.  Mix the peanut butter and maple syrup. Fold in the protein powder. 
2.  Mix to form a smooth dough. If it’s too dry, add 1-2 tbsp oat drink.
3.  Press into a lined loaf tin. Freeze for 30 minutes, then slice into 10 bars. 
4.  Drizzle the melted chocolate on top. Enjoy.  
    

![](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/wp-content/uploads/2024/02/Artboard-2-2-533x355.jpg)

**HOW MANY BARS CAN YOU MAKE?**
-------------------------------

You can make 10 bars with this recipe. Plenty to share or to stock-up for future use.
