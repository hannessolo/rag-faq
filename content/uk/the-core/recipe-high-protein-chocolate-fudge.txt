Recipe: High-Protein Chocolate Fudge

Looking for an indulgent protein boost? We’ve got just the treat for you. We have some more chocolatey protein goodness for you to enjoy with this super simple Chocolate Protein Fudge recipe.

**What are the macros and calories?**
-------------------------------------

*   177 Kcals
*   10g Protein
*   7g Carbs
*   13g Fat

**What are the three ingredients you need?**
--------------------------------------------

*   180g MACRO MUNCH™ Protein Chocolate
*   120g [**Smooth Peanut Butter**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/peanut-butter-1kg/bpf-pbut)
*   30g [**Chocolate Pure Whey Protein™**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/pure-whey-protein/bpb-wpc8-0000)
*   Optional: Rock salt

**How do you make it?**
-----------------------

*   Break and melt the chocolate and peanut butter for 30-60 seconds until the chocolate is fully melted.
*   Fold in the protein powder and mix until smooth.
*   Transfer into silicon moulds and refrigerate for 1 hour.
*   This recipe includes 10 servings.
