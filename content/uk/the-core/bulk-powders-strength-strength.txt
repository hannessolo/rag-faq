BULK POWDERS® | From Strength To Strength
=========================================

![International Fast Track](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/International-Fast-Track.jpg)

BULK POWDERS® are delighted to announce their elevation to the Sunday Times list of Britain’s private companies with the fastest-growing export sales – International Track 200.

Having achieved £15.5 million in international sales in 2017, BULK POWDERS® has entered the charts in the Top 200 at 61, with a two year growth of 66%.

“It’s a fantastic recognition of the progress we’ve made,” quoted Adam Rossiter, Co Founder and Joint Managing Director.  “We’ve spent a lot of time building our European teams and it’s a tribute to this very talented group that we’ve managed to make such impressive growth.”

The BULK POWDERS® value proposition – the best sports nutrition, wellness and functional foods at the best price – translates very easily to the European market.  The big difference is that the company now has much greater local knowledge to fine tune the offer.  This, combined with strong in year product innovation, has helped ensure that the progress will be carried forward into the next phase of growth.

About BULK POWDERS®
-------------------

BULK POWDERS® is Europe’s fastest growing supplier of sports supplements and nutritional products.  Launched in 2006, the business manufactures almost all products in-house and sells direct to consumers across the UK and Europe.

Founded by Elliot Dawes and Adam Rossiter, BULK POWDERS® has registered exponential growth over the last eleven years. BULK POWDERS® is a trading name of Sports Supplements Ltd, a company registered in England & Wales under the Number 05654661.
