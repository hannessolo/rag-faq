Recipe: High-Protein Coffee Milkshake
=====================================

What if drinking your daily coffee could also contribute to your protein goals? With [**protein coffee**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/collagen-coffee/bpf-pcof), now it can.

Discover this recipe for a high-protein coffee-flavoured milkshake, ideal for a pre-workout bump or afternoon boost. Whether you’re an athlete looking to jazz up their [protein shakes](https://www.bulk.com/uk/protein.html) or a casual gym-goer looking to up their protein, this recipe allows you to enjoy a healthy protein packed coffee delight. Coffee plus protein powder is the power-couple you need in your life.

You can find out all about the [**benefits of protein coffee**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/what-is-protein-coffee-benefits-uses/) in our blog.

**What are the macros and calories?**
-------------------------------------

*   27.6g Protein 
*   39g Carbs
*   15g Fat
*   366 Kcals 

**What ingredients do you need?**
---------------------------------

*   350ml Oat Drink or Milk
*   1 scoop Iced Latte Pure Whey Protein™
*   1 shot of espresso
*   1 tbsp Almond Butter
*   2 tsp Zero Calorie Barista Syrup
*   4 large ice cubes
*   Optional toppings: light whipping cream
