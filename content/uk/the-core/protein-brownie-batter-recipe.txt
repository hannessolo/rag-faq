Protein Brownie Batter Recipe
=============================

[03rd May 202228th November 2024](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/protein-brownie-batter-recipe/)

[Recipes](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/diet-and-nutrition/recipes/)

![](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/wp-content/uploads/2022/05/blog-image-vegan-protein-brownie-batter-1012x674.jpg)

Looking for a sustainable way to bump up your protein? Enter our Vegan Protein Brownie Batter. Soft, smooth and gooey. Our Gourmet Vegan Bar and Almond Butter are united in a match made in heaven. Plant-based decadence that’s simple to make (and even easier to eat). Just 253 kcals. Seeing really is believing. 

Ingredients
-----------

*   35g Bulk™ Cacao Powder
*   3 scoops (100g) [Bulk™ Vanilla Vegan Protein Powder](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/vegan-protein-powder/vser-vppo)
*   3 tablespoons [Bulk™ Ultra Fine Oats](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/ultra-fine-scottish-oats/bpb-oats-0000)
*   3 tablespoons [Bulk™ Maple Syrup Zero Calorie Syrup](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/zero-calorie-syrup/bpf-zcsy)
*   215ml Bulk™ Oat Drink
*   110g [Bulk™ Almond Butter](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/roasted-almond-butter/bpf-abut)
*   1 [Bulk™ Dark Chocolate Salted Caramel Gourmet Vegan Bar](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/gourmet-vegan-bar/vser-vgba)
*   1 [Bulk™ Cookies & Cream Gourmet Vegan Bar](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/gourmet-vegan-bar/vser-vgba)
*   15g walnut halves

Method
------

1.  Mix the protein powder, cacao and oats in a large bowl.
2.  Add the almond butter, maple syrup and milk.
3.  Mix to form a cake batter consistency.
4.  Fold in the chopped gourmet vegan bars and walnuts.
5.  Scoop out and enjoy!
