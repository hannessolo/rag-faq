Protein Gummies Recipe
======================

[05th September 202225th November 2024](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/recipe-high-protein-vegan-gummy-sweets/)

[Recipes](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/diet-and-nutrition/recipes/)

![protein gummies](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/1-1012x674.jpg)

Love wine gums? Let’s add some protein to that. Here we show you how to create some low-calorie, protein gummies. With just 34 calories and 8g of protein, these are an ideal snacking choice for the sweet-lovers amongst you. And did we mention they’re vegan too?

**What are the macros and calories (per protein gummy)?**
---------------------------------------------------------

*   34 Kcals
*   8g Protein
*   0g Fat
*   1g Carbs

![](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/IMG_2937-246x355.jpg)

**How do you make protein gummies?**
------------------------------------

*   Mix 125ml water and 1 tsp agar agar in a pan (off heat) until dissolved. Mix in 1 scoop of protein powder.
*   Cook on a medium heat until the protein is completely dissolved.
*   Transfer into silicon moulds and leave to cool slightly. Transfer to the fridge and set for 1 hour.
*   Repeat with the second flavour.

![](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/IMG_2938-267x355.jpg)

**What ingredients do you need to make protein gummies?**
---------------------------------------------------------

*   250ml water
*   2 tsp agar agar powder 
*   2 scoops [**Clear Vegan Protein**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/clear-vegan-protein/vser-cveg) (2 flavours of choice)
