### Instructions

1.) Whisk up your ingredients, everything except the coconut oil.

2.) Using a non-stick pan on medium- high heat start making your pancakes. Add tiny bits of coconut oil as you go along instead of all at once.

3.) They will cook quite quickly, so be ready to flip them over.

4.) Combine with a no-cal sauce for a macro-friendly pancake stack.

Recipe yields 10-12 pancakes (2 servings)
