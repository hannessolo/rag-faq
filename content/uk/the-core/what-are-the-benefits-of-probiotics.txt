What are Probiotics?
--------------------

[Probiotics](https://www.bulk.com/uk/health-wellbeing/probiotics) support good gut health by increasing concentrations of friendly bacteria.

They are ideal for anyone looking to improve digestion and to provide relief to the symptoms of intestinal disorders or discomforts which can be an issue for many.

Probiotics can be found naturally in some fermented dairy products, however the amount of beneficial bacteria in these foods is often questioned, and for this reason, people may decide to supplement with specific probiotic products. This also means your diet wouldn’t need to be changed drastically.

What are the benefits of Probiotics**?**
----------------------------------------

*   Helps to improve digestion
*   Can also help to support immune health
*   Nearly 8 Billion colony forming units (CFU)
*   Combination of probiotics and digestive enzymes
