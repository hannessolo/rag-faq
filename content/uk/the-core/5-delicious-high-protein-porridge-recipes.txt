Filling, hearty and so satisfying, [protein porridge](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/complete-protein-porridge/bble-cppo) is a breakfast staple for many. It’s high in fibre, richly nutritious, and has slow-release energy properties –  an ideal meal for fitness enthusiasts looking to keep calories low and energy output high.

Whether it’s a warm bowl on a frosty winter morning topped with syrup and nuts, or some fresh overnight oats sprinkled with sweet berries, the variations for porridge are vast. To commemorate World Porridge Day, we’ve put together five delicious high-protein porridge recipes. We’ve kept them as simple as possible, so you can easily recreate these at home. 

**1\. Chocolate Shell Overnight Protein Oats**
----------------------------------------------

Made using our [**protein powder**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/protein.html), this decadent high-protein chocolate cake recipe can be made super easy and super quick. See below how to make it and what ingredients you need.

### **What ingredients do you need?**

*   40g rolled oats
*   1 scoop Chocolate Peanut [**Pure Whey Protein™**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/pure-whey-protein.html)
*   100ml milk of choice
*   3 tbsp fat free yoghurt
*   1 tbsp Salted Caramel [**Flavoured Peanut Butter**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/flavoured-peanut-butters/bpf-pbut-flav)
*   20g dark chocolate, melted

### **How do you make it?**

*   Mix the oats, [**protein powder**](http://v) and milk.
*   In a separate bowl, mix the yoghurt and peanut butter. Spread over the oats.
*   Melt the chocolate in the microwave, then pour on top. 
*   Refrigerate overnight and enjoy the next morning. 

**2\. Anthony Joshua’s Overnight Oats**
---------------------------------------

Discover AJ’s very own overnight oats recipe, packed full of protein goodness to help you recover and prepare for heavy training.

“This is something I have in the morning after a heavy training session. But something I have to prepare the night before.” _Anthony Joshua_

### **What ingredients do you need?**

*   [**Oat Drink**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/oat-drink.html)
*   Caramel Latte [**Informed Whey® Protein**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/informed-whey/bpps-iwhe)
*   Porridge Oats

*   [**Dried Goji Berries**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/dried-goji-berries.html)

*   [**Organic Cacao Powder**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/foods)

*   [**Zero Calorie Syrup™**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/zero-calorie-syrup.html)

### **How do you make it?**

*   Pour the Oat Drink into a shaker
*   Add 2 scoops of Informed Whey® Protein
*   Shake for a few seconds
*   Add the mix to standard porridge oats
*   Mix in the Organic Cacao Powder
*   Add some Dried Goji Berries
*   Squeeze some Zero Calorie Syrup on top
*   Give it a final stir
*   Leave the mixture in the fridge overnight and enjoy the next morning

**3\. Chocolate Porridge Sacher** 
----------------------------------

If you want to elevate your breakfast game to new levels, give this Chocolate Porridge Sacher a go. Delicious, chocolatey and vegan. 

![](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/wp-content/uploads/2022/10/Chocolate-Porridge-Sacher-1-237x355.jpg)

### **What ingredients do you need?**

*   40g oats
*   10g [**Organic Cacao Powder**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/organic-cacao-powder.html)
*   10g Chocolate [**Soya Protein Isolate**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/soya-protein-isolate-90.html)
*   5g [**Chia Seeds**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/chia-seeds.html)
*   150g [**Oat Drink**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/oat-drink.html)
*   100g soya yoghurt
*   10g Chocolate Brownie [**Flavoured Peanut Butter**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/flavoured-peanut-butters/bpf-pbut-flav)
*   **Chocolate Whey Balls™**

### **How do you make it?**

Stir the following ingredients into a small saucepan over high heat until thickened:

*   40g oats
*   10g Organic Cacao Powder
*   10g Chocolate Soya Protein Isolate
*   5g Chia Seeds
*   150g Oat Drink

Then, pour into a bowl and add apricot jam and the cream made with:

*   100g soya yoghurt
*   10g Chocolate Brownie Flavoured Peanut Butter
*   10g Organic Cacao Powder

Finally, top with the Chocolate Whey Balls™.

**4\. Double Chocolate Baked Oats**
-----------------------------------

Did you know porridge oats could be baked? Thought as much. Try this indulgently chocolate high-protein porridge recipe.

![](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/wp-content/uploads/2022/10/Double-Chocolate-Baked-Oats-1-355x355.jpg)

### **What ingredients do you need?**

*   25g oats
*   25g oat flour
*   15g [**Pure Whey Protein™**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/pure-whey-protein.html)
*   1 tsp [**Organic Cacao Powder**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/organic-cacao-powder.html)
*   120ml unsweetened almond milk
*   1 heaped tsp White Chocolate Hazelnut [**Protein Spread**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/protein-spreads/bpf-pspr)
*   2 squares dark chocolate

### **How do you make it?**

*   Mix all ingredients (apart from the protein spread and chocolate) until combined
*   Pour ¾ of the mixture into a ramekin and add the protein spread.
*   Cover with the remaining mixture and top with the chocolate.
*   Bake for 15 minutes at 200C.

**5\. Super Simple Overnight Oats** 
------------------------------------

Overnight oats – this small but mighty breakfast option is the perfect grab-and-go meal for an active lifestyle. Prep it the night before, and thank yourself in the morning. All you need to do is follow the super-easy steps from our overnight oats recipe video below.

### **What ingredients do you need?**

*   50g oats
*   100ml milk (or milk alternative)
*   One scoop of [**Pure Whey Protein™**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/pure-whey-protein.html)
*   [**Dried Goji Berries**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/dried-goji-berries/bpf-goji) for topping

### **How do you make it?**

*   Mix the oats, whey protein and milk
*   Leave in the fridge overnight
*   Top as desired, perhaps with some Zero Calorie Syrup.
*   Eat and enjoy.
