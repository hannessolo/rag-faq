Protein Banana Bread Recipe
===========================

Love banana bread? Love [**protein**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/protein)? Here we come the two and create an amazing protein banana bread recipe. Just 191 calories per serving and 11g of protein, these sweet treats are a great option to brighten up your diet.

**What are the macros and calories for protein banana bread?**
--------------------------------------------------------------

*   11.1g Protein
*   23.1g Carbs
*   7.5 Fat
*   191 Kcals

![](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/IMG_2948-e1692113858224-249x355.jpg)

**What ingredients do you need for protein banana bread?**
----------------------------------------------------------

*   135g [**Ultra Fine Oats**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/ultra-fine-scottish-oats.html)
*   60g [**Pure Whey Protein™**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/pure-whey-protein.html) (Vanilla or Banana) 
*   1 ½ tsp baking powder
*   300g ripe bananas (3 large bananas), mashed
*   2 medium eggs
*   65g [**Peanut Butter**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/peanut-butter-1kg.html)
*   Optional: [**Protein Spread**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/protein-spreads/bpf-pspr) to top.

![](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/IMG_2950-233x355.jpg)

**How do you make high-protein banana bread?**
----------------------------------------------

*   Mix the oats, protein powder and baking powder. Add the mashed bananas, eggs and peanut butter. Whisk to combine.
*   Transfer to a loaf tin lined with baking paper. Bake for 35-40 minutes at 180C/160C fan.
*   When making banana bread with protein powder, make sure you leave to cool completely before topping with the spread.
