Unsure about what to buy for your fitness-fanatic friend this holiday season? We don’t blame you. Finding the perfect Christmas gift for each of your loved ones can be really hard, especially this year. So many of us already have an at-home workout space sorted, are living day-to-day in our favourite athleisure wear and are tuned into some kind of fitness tech accessory. Luckily, we’ve rounded up the best fitness and health holiday gift ideas from our range to guide you. These top picks are sure to please even the ultimate fitness enthusiast.

![macro-munch-protein-bar-tiramisu-box-of-12](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/macro-munch-protein-bar-tiramisu-box-of-12-62g-mmun-pbar-300x300.png)

**A box of 12 MACRO MUNCH™ bars**
---------------------------------

With all of the festivities this time of year, sticking to a health and fitness regime becomes so much more difficult. A great way to help make healthy food choices is to have healthy snacks on hand. Our MACRO MUNCH™  Protein Bars are a soft-baked, high-protein, low-sugar, triple-layered wonder. Packed with 20g of protein and low in sugar, they’re perfect for snacking on the go or as an extra source of nutrition between meals, available in 5 delicious flavours. This is the perfect stocking filler or secret Santa gift for health-conscious friends to join in on the sweet snacks.

**Stainless steel shaker 750ml** 
---------------------------------

A stylish, yet durable, shaker bottle to mix and transport supplements for all of the movers and shakers out there. Our premium shaker bottle is sure to put a smile on any health enthusiast’s face. Thanks to its stainless steel construction, the shake bottle is extremely durable, perfect for throwing into any gym bag. We love this as a gift for any active person to keep drinks cooler for longer and replace the tired plastic shakers and bottles we all know too well.

**Vegan protein brownie** 
--------------------------

Something for our plant-powered friends. Our Vegan Protein Brownie is a crowd favourite amongst everyone – not just for those on a vegan diet. With the texture and flavour of a traditional brownie, we use pea protein to make this treat sure to please any health and wellness guru. As with the rest of our Vegan Series™, Vegan Protein Brownies are free from artificial sweeteners. The sweetness comes from a mixture of oat syrup, date syrup and brown sugar. Each brownie provides 15g protein, 3g fibre and a lot of flavour, along with some genuine chocolate chips.

**Chocolate Protein Bites****™** 
---------------------------------

The perfect alternative to the box of chocolate that will sit in the cupboard until next year. Our Chocolate Protein Bites™ combine the most luxurious chocolate with the highest quality Whey Protein to deliver a ridiculously tasty, high protein snack. Small crisp pieces are folded into the chocolate, ensuring that each mouthful is packed with taste and texture. Unlike your standard sweet tooth satisfaction, Chocolate Protein Bites™ deliver an impressive 30% protein content.

**Peanut butter 1kg** 
----------------------

This is the gift that keeps on giving – because there is so much of it! Our entirely natural, 100% roasted peanut butter is the perfect gift for anyone to use in smoothies and cooking, as a spread or even from the spoon when no one’s looking. What’s best is it comes in both crunchy and smooth varieties, so there’s something for everyone- and it’s easy to wrap. 

Maintaining our health, fitness and wellbeing has never been more crucial. Whether it’s a healthy home chef, sport enthusiast or any active being you’re buying for, we have you covered this holiday season.
