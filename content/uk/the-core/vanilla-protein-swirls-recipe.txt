Vanilla Protein Swirls Recipe
=============================

[Recipes](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/diet-and-nutrition/recipes/)

Here’s a quick and easy **Vanilla Protein Swirls recipe**, perfect if you’re on a low carb diet but need a fast supply of high quality **protein and fats**. Each serving delivers a whopping 49.5g protein, plus healthy fats and omega 3’s. Try it yourself!

### Instructions:

1.) Heat up the **coconut oil** in a non-stick pan on a medium heat. While it is melting, whisk up the **egg whites** with the protein powder and add to the pan.

2.) Cook for 2 minutes, then sprinkle the **chia seeds** & cinnamon on top, and scatter the almond butter on evenly. Add a lid and cook for another minute.

3.) Take the pan off the heat and roll the pancake up carefully. Cut into 5/6 pieces and serve on their side. Top with agave nectar or some honey for sweetness.
