Cake, anyone? Cure your cravings in under a minute with our quick and easy Microwave Mug Cake recipe. It’s no secret that everyone loves cake and there is no reason why we should have to give it up when trying to maintain a healthy active lifestyle. Our Protein Mug Cake mix provides this recipe with as much as 24g protein and as little as 2.7g fat per serving – hitting your protein requirements has never been so delicious! Get creative and try adding in some fresh fruit, seeds, nuts and chocolate drops to mix in with your cake.

Ingredients:

2 scoops of [Protein Mug Cake Mix](https://www.bulk.com/uk/protein-mug-cake-mix.html)  
Handful of blueberries  
70ml of water  
A pinch of cocoa powder

Method:

Mix ingredients together in a large mug  
Microwave for 40 – 50 seconds until cake rises  
Dust with cocoa powder  
Grab a spoon and enjoy!

Macros (per serving):

188 kcal  
24g protein  
16g carbs  
2.7g fat
