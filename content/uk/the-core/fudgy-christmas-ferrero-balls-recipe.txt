**Instructions:**

1.) Mix all the wet ingredients in a bowl first, then add in the dry ingredients, except for the biscuits.

2.) Then, roll your mix into balls and roll around in your gluten-free biscuits. Pressing them in if you need to.

3.) Voila, easy peasy, fudgy ferrero balls. Perfect treat to impress your dinner guests over xmas!
