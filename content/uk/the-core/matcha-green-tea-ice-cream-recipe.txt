### Instructions:

1.) In a high speed blender, add all your ingredients except for the Matcha Green Tea Powder, add that in last.

2.) Pour the mix into a bowl/container and stick in the freezer for approx. 4-5 hours, stirring it 3-4 times within that period to make sure it remains ‘creamy’

3.) Grab you ice-cream spoon and… well… eat it ALL!

Chocolate is optional but it gave it a nice touch!

Note: For a high protein version add ½ scoop Vanilla Casein or Vegan Vanilla Protein Blend. For a lower carb alternative, omit the sweet freedom or replace with a lower sugar content ingredient.
