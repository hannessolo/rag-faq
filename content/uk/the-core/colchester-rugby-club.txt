BULK POWDERS™ joins forces with Colchester Rugby Club
=====================================================

[19th October 201225th August 2022](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/colchester-rugby-club/)

[News and Updates](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/news-and-updates/)

![New Flavours](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/120914_Blog-Placeholder.jpg)

BULK POWDERS™ has famously never made a big deal of sponsorship.  We are regularly asked for deals by a number of clubs.  And we also think “Why would we pay someone to put our brand on their shirt?”, if in doing so we make our products more expensive for our customers.

But that does not mean we don’t like to back our own product.  And rugby, of all the great team sports, is one sport which really tests all our claims: for strength, endurance, focus and recovery. And we have really been putting our products to the test.

Colchester 1st XV are a great example of a motivated, ambitious young side fighting their way out of London 1 North.  Typically, they are both younger and smaller than their opponents.  And up until now they have not had a structured approach to sports nutrition.  And that’s where we come in.

As part of our product development, we have put one of our nutritionists onto producing the perfect supplement support for the team.  On top of that, we will be working on some classic rugby case studies, trying to work on individual stories that we hope are representative.  You will be able to follow our progress online, both for the team and the lucky individuals as the season unfolds.

In parallel, we have launched our [rugby bundle](https://www.bulk.com/uk/sports-nutrition/stacks-bundles.html "Rugby Bundle") (alright, it’s a stack but our rugby friends don’t do stacks).  It gives a month’s supply and provides fantastic value at only £44.99.  We may develop more as the season progresses.

In the meantime, watch out for our feedback and let’s get Colchester promoted.
