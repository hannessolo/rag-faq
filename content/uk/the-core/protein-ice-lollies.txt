Keep your cool post-workout with these Clear Whey Ice Lollies. With just 3 ingredients you’ll have yourself the perfect protein packed summer treat.

### Ingredients:

*   150g fresh strawberries
*   40g [Clear Whey Isolate](https://www.bulk.com/uk/clear-whey-isolate.html)
*   Cherry Bomb flavour
*   200g milk/water for an icy consistency or 200g for a thick and creamy consistency

### Method:

1.  Blend all ingredients together
2.  Pour the mixture in 4 ice lolly moulds (or⁣ more/less according to the size)⁣
3.  Freeze for a minimum of two hours and summer is served
