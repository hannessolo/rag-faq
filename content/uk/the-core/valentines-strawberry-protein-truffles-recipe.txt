Here’s something you’re bound to fall in love with this Valentine’s Day. Whether you need a last minute present for your loved one, or just fancy treating yourself, our Strawberry Protein Truffles by [Healthy Twenties](http://www.healthytwenties.co.uk/) are the perfect way to get that heart fluttering!

**INSTRUCTIONS**:

1\. In a bowl, combine the protein powder and coconut flour. Then add in the almond milk, one tablespoon at a time until the mixture sticks together.

2\. Shape into balls and then place in the freezer for 10 minutes to firm.

3\. Melt the chocolate and drizzle on top of the truffles. Leave to cool in the fridge until the chocolate has set. Enjoy!

Recipe makes approx. 6 truffles.
