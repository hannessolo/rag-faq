Fudgy Chocolate Brownie Recipe
==============================

[09th July 201926th November 2024](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/fudgy-chocolate-brownie-recipe/)

[Health & Wellbeing](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/healthwellbeing/)

![](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/IMG_0540-899x674.jpg)

**Ingredients:**

[65g almond butter](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/roasted-almond-butter/bpf-abut)  
70g coconut sugar or brown sugar  
60g maple syrup  
[50g melted coconut oil](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/organic-virgin-coconut-oil/bpf-ovco)  
55g unsweetened cacao powder  
[1 teaspoon vanilla LiquiFlav flavour drops](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/liquiflav/bp-liqu-50ml)   
[¼ teaspoon salt](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/himalayan-pink-salt/bpf-psal-hima)   
2 eggs  
35g oat flour

**Method:**

Pre-heat an oven to 180\*C.

Add all the ingredients to a large mixing bowl.

MIX WELL.

Pour the mixture into a lined brownie tin and bake for about 15-20 mins.

Leave to cool. Cut and enjoy!
