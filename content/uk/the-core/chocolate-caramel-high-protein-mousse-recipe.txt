Chocolate Caramel High Protein Mousse Recipe
============================================

![Chocolate Caramel High Protein Mousse](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/wp-content/uploads/2016/06/chocolate-caramel-high-protein-mousse-recipe.jpg)

This will go down as one of the simplest recipes ever made and probably one of the tastiest! With 14g protein and 4g fibre per serving, this **Chocolate Caramel High Protein Mousse** is a great snack that is quick and easy to make and will keep you feeling full…until the next bowl.

### Instructions:

1.) Mix all ingredients in a food processor and pour into glasses/bowls.

2.) Place in the fridge for at least two hours before serving!
