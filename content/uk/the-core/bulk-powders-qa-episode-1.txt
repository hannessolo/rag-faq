In this first edition of [BULK POWDERS™ Q&A](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/training-and-exercise/qa/ "BULK POWDERS™ Q&A"), a new weekly training series, sponsored athlete Luke Sumner-Wilson answers questions about training arms, breaking through plateaus and meal frequency. If you any questions for Luke please ask us via social #ASKBULK or comment below and Luke will answer 3 new questions every week.

What’s covered in episode #1 of BULK POWDERS™ Q&A?
--------------------------------------------------

1.  — How many times a week should I be training arms?
2.  — What is the best way to break through a weightloss plateau?
3.  — How many meals a day should I be having?

**NB:** Due to the broad nature of some of the questions, Luke may expand on some of his answers in future Q&A videos when the context of discussion is better understood. Try to be as specific as you can when posting questions to avoid generic answers.
