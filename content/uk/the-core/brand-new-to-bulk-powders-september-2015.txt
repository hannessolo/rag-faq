**Brand new to BULK POWDERS™**

**_New Additions_**

One of BULK POWDERS™ latest launches to hit the range in September was **[Coconut Water Powder,](../../coconut-water-powder.html)** or more commonly referred to as ‘Natures Isotonic Drink’. **Coconut Water Powder** is a natural way of supporting performance and replenishing the key salts that are lost during an intense workout. The powder is particularly high in magnesium, potassium and vitamin C, making it perfect for those wanting a natural electrolyte solution. What’s even better is that it is the first ever flavoured coconut water powder on the market. It has a subtle yet pleasant coconut flavour to mask any saltiness that most other coconut waters will provide.

[![coconutwater](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/wp-content/uploads/2015/10/coconutwater.jpg)](https://www.bulk.com/uk/coconut-water-powder.html)

Another new addition to the PURE SERIES™ range this month was our [**Garcinia Cambogia Capsules**](../../garcinia-cambogia-capsules-500mg.html). Sure, nothing that new or exciting to the industry, however, these capsules contain over 500mg of extract from the Garcinia Cambogia Wholefruit and are one of the purest on the market. They also come in an easy-to-consume capsule for optimal convenience, and are free of any stimulants such as caffeine.

[![garciniacombogia](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/wp-content/uploads/2015/10/garciniacombogia.jpg)](https://www.bulk.com/uk/garcinia-cambogia-capsules-500mg.html)

First of all we have the **[BULK POWDERS™ Jogging Bottoms](../../flex-fit-joggers-uk.html)** which are perfect for training in or even everyday casual wear. We have ensured a heavyweight material when designing these jogging bottoms, for the most premium and comfortable feel. The legs are also cuffed at the bottom for maximal mobility when you train.
