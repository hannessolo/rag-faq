### Instructions

1.) Melt your coconut oil and then proceed to stir in the ingredients as listed, one by one using a whisk at first (for wet ingredients) then a spoon when a batter starts to form.

2.) Then fill your mould with the batter, it should be quite easy to handle. I used my fingers to press in.

3.) Stick in the freezer for about an hour until ready to pop from mould.

4.) Recipe yielded 10 mini hearts.
