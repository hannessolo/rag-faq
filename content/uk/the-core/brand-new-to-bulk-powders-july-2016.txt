At BULK POWDERS™ we pride ourselves on our product development and being at the forefront of new product innovation in [**Sports Nutrition**](../../sports-nutrition.html). This doesn’t mean getting any old product out there though and when looking to design a new formulation for our customers we ensure that the focus is solely on the quality and integrity of each and every product.

This involves all aspects of the product including; raw ingredient sourcing, final formulation, flavour and even the product packaging. Whether it is a health food in our [**Active Foods**](../../shop-by-range/active-foods.html)™ range, a vitamin and mineral blend or a product targeting elite athletes, our number one goal stays the same – to provide the very best product for our customer’s needs.

The last few months have rounded up some fantastic launches for BULK POWDERS™ expanding the whole brand, as well as focusing on key developments in each of our individual ranges. In case you missed any of these, below is a round-up of a few of the main launches…
