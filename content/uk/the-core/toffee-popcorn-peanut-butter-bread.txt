### Summary

A great tasting toffee popcorn peanut butter bread with [Pure Whey Protein](../../shop-by-category/protein-supplements/pure-whey-protein.html)™ (toffee popcorn) at its core. Fantastic on its own or with a little butter or jam. Really hit the spot!

### **Instructions**

1.) Pre heat the oven to 180 degrees C.

2.) Mix together the peanut butter, eggs, apple cider vinegar and almond milk.

3.) Add in the whey, baking powder and [coconut flour](../../coconut-flour.html) and mix to form a thick batter.

4.) Pour the batter into a silicone bread pan and bake for 30 minutes until baked throughout the loaf.

5.) Leave to cool before slicing.
