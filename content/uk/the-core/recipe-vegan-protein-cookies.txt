Recipe: Vegan protein cookies
=============================

[Recipes](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/diet-and-nutrition/recipes/)

![](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/wp-content/uploads/2022/12/Vegan-protein-cookies-1012x674.jpg)

A great alternative to traditional cookies, vegan protein cookies are are created with plant-based protein sources that can help support muscle growth and repair. 

Here we share a special recipe for creating vegan protein cookies at home. Check out the macros, ingredients and method below.

**What are the macros and calories?**
-------------------------------------

*   256 kcals
*   19G Protein
*   15g Fat
*   14g Carbs
*   5g Fibre

This recipe serves 2.

**What ingredients do you need?**
---------------------------------

*   What you need:
*   1 tbsp milled flaxseed
*   3 tbsp water 
*   2 tbsp Chocolate Gourmet Vegan Protein
*   1 tbsp Organic Cacao Powder
*   2 tbsp Salted Caramel Flavoured [Peanut Butter](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/peanut-butter-1kg/bpf-pbut)
*   1 tbsp Oat Drink
*   2-4 drops Vanilla LiquiFlav™
*   1 Gourmet Vegan Bar, cut into small chunks

**How do you make it?**
-----------------------

*   Mix the milled flaxseed and water. Leave to set for 10-15 mins. 
*   Mix the protein and cacao. Add the set flaxseed mixture, peanut butter, milk and flavour drops. 
*   Divide into 2 equal balls. Press down on a lined baking sheet. Top with the gourmet vegan bar chunks. 
*   Bake for 15 minutes at 180C/160C fan. Leave to cool and enjoy!
