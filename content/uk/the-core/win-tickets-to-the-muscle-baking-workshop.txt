WIN! – Take your seats at The Muscle Bakery Workshop
----------------------------------------------------

On Saturday November 21st in London, [The Muscle Baker](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/tag/muscle-bakery/) will be running The Muscle Baking Workshop in partnership with ourselves, GYMversus, Wheyhey and CocoPro. What a line up.

### Agenda

Meet & Greet  
Introduction to ingredients  
Basic substitutions  
DIY ingredients  
Cookie & Protein Bar making  
Cake, Bakes & Breads  
No-Bake Treats  
Some photo’s (for Instagram of course)  
EAT TIME!  
Freebies & Prizes.  
(Then unfortunately home time)

Everyone who attends will receive an exclusive printed The Muscle Bakery recipe book (not available until the end of November), freebies from the sponsors, stickers, certificates, aprons and the chance to win some Muscle Bakery treats.
