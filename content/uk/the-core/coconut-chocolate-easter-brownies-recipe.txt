Coconut Chocolate Easter Brownies Recipe
========================================

Instructions
------------

1.) Pre-heat oven to 160 Degrees C.

2.) Melt chocolate and coconut oil in microwave or in a glass bowl over a pan of boiling water.

3.) Fold in the remaining ingredients until well combined.

4.) Pour mix into a lined baking tray and bake for 10-12mins.

5.) Leave to cool completely before frosting.

6.) Frost using nut butter and mini eggs, however you can top with whatever you fancy.

7.) Try any of our [nut butters](https://www.bulk.com/uk/foods/nut-butters.html) for the perfect frosting!
