### Summary:

This is a great recipe if you have a sweet tooth and need to watch your calorie intake. Snack on these peppermint thins guilt free at less than 21 calories per piece. If you like minty chocolates, youâ€™ll love these.

### Instructions:

1.) Melt your coconut oil, then whisk in the rest of your ingredients.

2.) Pour into a large square dish/mould prepared with greaseproof paper and place in freezer for an hour or so.

3.) Once it has hardened, remove from freezer and slice into squares.

4.) Eat some then save the rest to impress your friends when they ask for after dinner mints!
