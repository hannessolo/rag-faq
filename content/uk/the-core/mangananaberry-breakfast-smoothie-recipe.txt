Mangananaberry Breakfast Smoothie Recipe
========================================

[22nd February 201629th July 2022](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/mangananaberry-breakfast-smoothie-recipe/)

[Recipes](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/diet-and-nutrition/recipes/)

![Mangananaberry Breakfast Smoothie](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/mangananaberry-breakfast-smoothie-recipe.jpg)

Sophie, of [100 Healthy Days](http://www.100-healthy-days.com), received a hamper of goodies containing our [Pure Whey Protein](../../pure-whey-protein.html)™, [Ultra Fine Scottish Oats](../../ultra-fine-scottish-oats.html) and **Liquid Egg Whites**. Sophie trains early in the morning, so wanted to create something that was quick and easy to throw together, perfect as a quick breakfast before an AM CrossFit session. Her creation has been christened the ‘**Mangananaberry Breakfast Smoothie**‘.

**Method:**

Add all ingredients to a blender, blend, and you’re done!
