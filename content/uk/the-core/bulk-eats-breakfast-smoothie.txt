Bored of the same old protein shake day in, day out? It’s time to mix it up with our breakfast smoothie recipe – quite literally! After all, breakfast is the most important meal of the day so, we decided to give it a special treatment. Get scrolling to find out more about the ingredients and check out our handy tutorial for some extra help.

No matter what your fitness goals are, protein intake is an important part of achieving the targets you set yourself. Breakfast smoothies are a great way to start the day, packed with everything you need in one quick and easy recipe – think high protein, high calcium and low fat with no cooking skills required. 

This tasty breakfast smoothie recipe features our [Pure Whey Protein™](https://www.bulk.com/uk/pure-whey-protein.html) and all-natural [Peanut Butter](https://www.bulk.com/uk/peanut-butter-1kg.html) to pack in all of the protein and flavour. The beauty of a breakfast smoothie is that the flavour combinations are endless and you can customise it to suit any taste. Simply throw all of your ingredients into a blender, blend and enjoy!

Ingredients:

*   1 spoonful of [Peanut Butter](https://www.bulk.com/uk/peanut-butter-1kg.html)
*   A handful of mixed berries
*   1 frozen banana
*   1 scoop of [Pure Whey Protein™](https://www.bulk.com/uk/pure-whey-protein.html)
*   (You could also use [Clear Whey Isolate)](https://www.bulk.com/uk/clear-whey-isolate.html)
*   250ml of almond milk

Method:

*   Mix
*   Blend
*   Enjoy

Macros:

*   381 kcal
*   29g protein
*   45g carbs
*   11g fat
