Bulk™ Announced As No. 34 In The Sunday Times 2017 Virgin Fast Track 100
========================================================================

[15th December 201725th August 2022](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/bulk-powders-announced-no-34-sunday-times-2017-virgin-fast-track-100/)

[News and Updates](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/news-and-updates/)

Leading Sports Nutrition brand BULK POWDERS® has achieved another fantastic year of growth & been placed No. 34 in The 2017 [Sunday Times Virgin Fast Track 100](http://www.fasttrack.co.uk/company_profile/bulk-powders-4/) – Britain’s top 100 private Companies with the fastest growing sales over the last 3 years.

**4th Highest Ranking Ecommerce Retailer.**

Despite The Sunday Times Virgin Fast Track 100 becoming more competitive every year, BULK POWDERS® has managed to move up 4 places to No. 34 in 2017, making them the 4th highest ranking ecommerce retailer in the list as well as the top sports nutrition brand.

**Revenue Approaching £35m in 2017.**

BULK POWDERS® was started by Adam Rossiter & Elliot Dawes in 2006 and has grown rapidly to be the No 2 Sports Nutrition brand in the UK (Revenue approaching £35m in 2017 & expecting to achieve in excess of £50m in 2018) with strong growth across Europe in 13 different countries. 2017 will see an industry leading 50 products & ranges launched.

BULK POWDERS® offers people the highest quality Sports Nutrition to put you on track for peak performance. Whether you’re a casual gym-goer or a seasoned pro, BULK POWDERS® will help you to achieve your personal best every day. With four key ranges, encapsulating the Pro, Pure & Complete Series, as well as Active Foods, BULK POWDERS® has products for every training goal and simply will not be beaten on price, quality or service.

**Signing Well Known Athletes & Ambassadors.**

BULK POWDERS® is not only expanding its range of products in the near future but very much focused on further driving brand awareness, equity & sales. This has included signing well known athletes & ambassadors to represent the brand including World Champion & Olympic diver Tom Daley, Lucy Watson (Vegan) and Lions & England Rugby star Jonathan Joseph.

Adam Rossiter, Managing Director BULK POWDERS® comments: “It’s fantastic news that we have moved up the Fast Track 100 to No. 34 this year. Being the highest ranked sports nutrition brand, as well as one of the UK’s overall top ranked eCommerce brands demonstrates how we have continued to evolve successfully as a business. We are very excited about achieving even more in 2018 & beyond”.
