What is Nuts About Whey™?
-------------------------

Nuts About Whey™ is an exciting new twist on nut butters. They are fortified with [Whey Protein Isolate](../../shop-by-category/protein-supplements/whey-protein/pure-whey-isolate-90.html "Whey Protein Isolate") and Coconut Oil and also come in our delicious BULK POWDERS™ Pure Whey Protein™ flavours!

With **over 50g protein per 100g** of Nuts About Whey™, along with **added Coconut Oil** and absolutely **no added salt, sugar or palm oil**, the new generation of nut butters have landed.

This delicious high protein nut butter can be used to spread on almost anything, as well as being a tasty addition to any protein shake.

If you’re addicted to our natural 1kg tubs of [Pistachio Butter](../../shop-by-category/foods/pistachio-butter.html "Natural Pistachio Butter"), [Peanut Butter](../../shop-by-category/foods/peanut-butter.html "Natural Peanut Butter"), [Almond Butter](../../shop-by-category/foods/almond-butter.html "Natural Almond Butter") and [Cashew Butter](../../shop-by-category/foods/cashew-butter.html "Natural Cashew Butter"), Nuts About Whey™ should definitely be on your shopping list too.

Right now, take your pick from the following Nuts About Whey™ flavours – **Chocolate Cookies**, **Chocolate**, **White Chocolate** and **Banana Fudge**.
