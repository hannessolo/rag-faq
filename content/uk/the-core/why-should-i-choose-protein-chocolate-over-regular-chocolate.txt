**What is protein chocolate?**
------------------------------

In order to understand what exactly protein chocolate is, we’ll take a deeper dive into which ingredients it contains. The main difference between protein milk chocolate and regular milk chocolate is, quite simply, the ingredients. 

The main ingredients of regular milk chocolate include milk, sugar, cocoa butter, cocoa mass and vegetable fats. Instead of milk, our protein chocolate contains whey protein concentrate and milk powder. And instead of sugar, our protein chocolate contains a sweetener called maltitol. 

**Reasons to try protein chocolate**
------------------------------------

If you want one main reason to try our MACRO MUNCH™ Protein Chocolate, the clue is in the name. Our chocolate contains a source of protein, which contributes to the growth and maintenance of muscle mass. This helps chocolate lovers enjoy their favourite sweet snack, while staying on track with their protein goals. 

Our protein chocolate, unlike regular milk chocolate, has no added sugars and only contains naturally occurring sugars. As an added bonus, it’s also a source of fibre. 

Many mainstream brands of milk chocolate also contain palm oil. So if you’re looking to choose a more sustainable option, our protein chocolate is palm oil free.

Aside from being a wiser choice, both nutritionally and environmentally, our protein chocolate tastes just as good as regular chocolate. We’ve achieved the same rich, chocolatey taste and velvety texture. Ready to give it a go? Take a look at the stats.
