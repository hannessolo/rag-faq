Train hard, recover strong.

The steps you take to recover after working out can be as important as the training itself. [High-protein smoothies](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/5-easy-high-protein-smoothie-recipes/) are a great option after a workout because they are easy to make and work quickly to start replenishing your energy stores.

This recovery smoothie recipe features our [Pure Whey Protein](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/pure-whey-protein/bpb-wpc8-0000) in chocolate, combined with Organic Cacao Powder and a hint of cinnamon for an indulgent post-workout protein hit. Add a spoonful of [chia seeds](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/chia-seeds/bpf-chia) for added texture and voila!

Our recovery smoothie is the perfect quick and easy way to refuel ready for your next workout! Simply follow the super-easy steps from our video recipe below – you’ll be sipping on this delicious drink in no time.

Ingredients:

*   1 scoop of [Pure Whey Protein](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/pure-whey-protein/bpb-wpc8-0000) in Chocolate
*   250ml of almond milk
*   1 banana
*   2 dates
*   1 scoop of [Organic Cacao Powder](https://www.bulk.com/uk/foods)
*   A pinch of cinnamon
*   1 spoonful of [Chia Seeds](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/chia-seeds/bpf-chia)

Method:

*   Mix
*   Blend
*   Enjoy!

Macros:

*   400 kcal
*   30g protein
*   54g carbs
*   10g fat
