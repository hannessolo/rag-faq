**What are the macros and calories?**
-------------------------------------

*   473 Kcals 
*   27g Protein
*   55g Carbs
*   17g Fat

This recipe serves 2.

**What ingredients do you need?**
---------------------------------

### **For the pancakes:**

*   4 scoops (100g) [**Vegan Protein Pancake Mix**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/vegan-protein-pancake-mix/vser-vpan)
*   100ml water
*   2 ripe bananas
*   1-2 tbsp Organic Virgin Coconut Oil (for frying)

### **For the peanut butter sauce:**

*   3 tbsp [**Powdered Peanut Butter**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/powdered-peanut-butter.html)
*   2.5 tbsp [**Oat Drink**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/oat-drink.html)
*   2 tsp Maple Syrup [**Zero Calorie Syrup™**](https://www.bulk.com/uk/zero-calorie-syrup.html)

**How do you make it?**
-----------------------

*   Mix the pancake mix and water to a smooth, thin batter. 
*   Slice the banana into thin discs, then dip into the batter to coat. 
*   Heat the coconut oil in a large frying pan over a medium heat. Fry each banana bite for a minute on each side or until golden brown.
*   Mix the ingredients for the peanut butter sauce. Drizzle over the pancake bites and tuck in.
