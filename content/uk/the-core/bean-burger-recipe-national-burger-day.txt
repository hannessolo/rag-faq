Bean Burger Recipe for National Burger Day
==========================================

[27th August 202027th November 2024](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/bean-burger-recipe-national-burger-day/)

[Recipes](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/diet-and-nutrition/recipes/)

![national burger day bean burger recipe](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/wp-content/uploads/2020/08/national-burger-day-bean-burger-recipe.jpeg)

Now here’s something worth celebrating this year. Our ambassador [Emily Solman](https://www.instagram.com/_emilysworld/) has put together this wonderful bean burger recipe and it totally deserves a spot in your calendar. There’s simply no better way to spend National Burger Day.

Ingredients
-----------

*   1 tin of black beans
*   Half red onion (diced)
*   1/4 cup of [Super Pea Protein Isolate](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/super-pea-protein-isolate/bpb-sppi-0000)
*   1 cup of [Ultra Fine Oats](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/ultra-fine-scottish-oats/bpb-oats-0000)
*   3 tbsp passata
*   2 tsp of garlic granules
*   1 tbsp spoon of smoked paprika
*   2 tsp of Active Seasonings in Smoke and Spice
*   200g of tomato pasta sauce
*   Salt and pepper

Method
------

*   Preheat oven to 200°C
*   Blend the black beans, then add them to a mixing bowl.
*   Add the remaining ingredients to the bowl and mix together.
*   Form the mixture into balls (makes 6) and flatten onto a lined baking tray.
*   Bake for 10 mins then turn them over.
*   Bake for another 10 mins.
*   Serve and enjoy!
