Anthony Joshua Announced As New Bulk Athlete
============================================

[19th December 201727th November 2024](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/anthony-joshua-announced-new-bulk-powders-athlete/)

[News and Updates](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/news-and-updates/)

![Anthony Joshua](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/Anthony-Joshua.jpg)

World & 2012 Olympic heavyweight boxing champion Anthony Joshua has become a BULK® athlete. As a brand athlete, Anthony will benefit from world class nutritional products to help him deliver outstanding performances in the ring.

The need to outperform the competition & always stay sharp in the ring is critical – and this is something that ex-Olympian Anthony Joshua knows more than most. As world heavyweight champion, Anthony always has to produce top performances to hold onto his belts. The BULK® range will help him do just that & lead him to maintain his unblemished 100% record in 2018 & beyond.

Anthony & BULK® share the same desire and ambition to be the best – this is why the partnership makes perfect sense. BULK POWDERS® offers people the highest quality Sports Nutrition to put you on track for peak performance. Whether you’re a casual gym-goer or a seasoned pro, BULK® will help you to achieve your personal bests every day.

Our Pro Series is one of four ranges that BULK POWDERS® offer, each handily categorised so it’s quick & simple to assess which will best meet a specific need. The Informed Sport accredited Pro Series includes premium [Whey Protein](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/pure-whey-protein/bpb-wpc8-0000), [Fat Burner](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/cutting-edge/bpps-cedg) & [Pre-Workout](https://www.bulk.com/uk/products/elevate/bpps-elev) and there are also other products available to help push Anthony to the peak of his natural abilities.

**Of the partnership, Anthony Joshua said:** “I’m delighted to be working with BULK®. As an athlete, I am always pushing to improve, and I know that having the right nutrition is key to unlocking my top performance in the ring. BULK POWDERS® are now my official sports nutrition supplier & they will help support me in achieving my targets in 2018 & beyond – and, importantly, I can easily incorporate the products into my busy routine. I can’t wait to see the results we can achieve together”.

**Elliot Dawes, Managing Director BULK said**: “We are absolutely delighted that Anthony Joshua has decided to become a BULK® athlete, especially at such a crucial time in his career. He is one of the world’s top athletes so its fantastic news. We are very much looking forward to working closely with him & supporting him through all of his nutritional needs ongoing”.

Anthony Joshua joins the team which includes Tom Daley, Lucy Watson & Jonathan Joseph as the latest BULK® athlete.
