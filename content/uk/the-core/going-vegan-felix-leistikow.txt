============================

![](https://www.bulk.com/uk/the-core/wp-content/uploads/2020/01/felix.leistikow_64531052_330148661208070_3411375670775068407_n-1-627x674.jpg)

Four of our ambassadors were given the challenge to go vegan for January. We caught up with Felix Leistikow to find out how he’s getting on!

**How are you finding it? Are you finding it hard?**

It’s surprisingly good! At the start it was hard to take animal based products off the shopping list, but it’s getting easier day by day.

**Have you noticed a difference in your energy levels?**

In the beginning I had a huge feeling of hunger even after I ate a huge meal, but after a while it felt really good not being so “full”.

**Are there any foods you’re missing?**

I really miss cheese. Although there are some dairy free options, they don’t really taste like actual cheese!

**Is it making you try different foods?**  
Yes, since I started I tried several different types of food I’ve never heard of before!

**Are you getting enough vitamins and minerals from the foods you’re eating? If not, are you supplementing these?** 

I don’t really know if I am, so I’ve been taking supplements like [Omega 369](https://www.bulk.com/uk/vegan-omega-3-ahiflower.html), [Vitamin D3](https://www.bulk.com/uk/vitamin-d3-tablets-5000iu.html) and [B12](https://www.bulk.com/uk/vitamin-b12-tablets-1000-mcg.html) and minerals like [Zinc](https://www.bulk.com/uk/optizinc-tablets.html) and [Magnesium](https://www.bulk.com/uk/magnesium-bisglycinate-tablets.html) from Bulk Powders.

**Will you carry it on post jan?**

I think I will take a step back to being a vegetarian. Being vegan might have been a too abrupt start to get into the whole world of plant based meals and maybe in the future I’ll go back to vegan.
