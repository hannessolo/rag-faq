We have an exciting opportunity for one lucky male and one lucky female; a chance to feature on BULK POWDERS™ website in an upcoming video promotion and be seen by over 300,000 people every month.

How to stand out.
-----------------

We’re looking for people who live and breathe fitness, so make sure this comes across in the video you submit. **Do something that makes you stand out from the rest**. Tell us why you want to be in our video. You could even show us your party trick. This isn’t a run-of-the-mill competition. This is a BIG DEAL.

### What you need to do.

All you need to do for a chance to feature on BULK POWDERS™ website is:

1.  Upload a short video to YouTube (max 1 minute) with your name and the hashtag **#RevealYourself**. It’s that simple. Let us know on email or social media the URL of your video, **OR**
2.  Email your video direct to [s.aldous@bulkpowders.com](mailto:s.aldous@bulkpowders.com) to confirm your entry.

### Conditions.

*   Strictly no X Factor sob stories. These entries will be placed on a small vessel, pushed out to sea and set alight with a flaming arrow.
*   Your entry must include your name and the hashtag **#RevealYourself**.
*   Entries must be submitted by 10am on Friday 19th June. The lucky winners will be notified by 9pm on Friday 19th June.
*   You must be a resident of the UK and over the age of 21.
*   You must be available on Wednesday 24th June from 9am – 5pm in Colchester, Essex.
*   Travel expenses will be covered.
*   You will receive a customised bundle of supplements on the day worth £100.

### Good luck!

The next time you **#RevealYourself** it might just change your life…
