Arginine FAQs
-------------

What is Arginine?

Arginine is a semi-essential amino acid involved in the creation of nitric oxide (NO), which helps to improve blood flow and muscular pump. It is popular as a pre-workout supplement.

What are the benefits of arginine supplements?

Arginine supplements can boost your intake of arginine when you need it most, typically just before a workout. Arginine helps to increase blood flow and muscular pumps during your workouts.

When should you take arginine?

Typically arginine should be taken as a pre-workout supplement.

What is arginine used for?

Arginine is popular as a pre-workout supplement as it helps to increase blood flow and muscular pumps by increasing nitric oxide (NO) production.
