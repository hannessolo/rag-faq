All in One Protein Supplements
==============================

category

sports-nutrition/all-in-one-supplements

For many of us, making a shake can often involve delving into multiple pouches of protein, carbohydrates, amino acids and more. After all, it is rare that we consume sports nutrition products in isolation; we often combine them together to make our own ‘All in One Supplements’.

A prime example of the need for an all in one supplement is [post-workout](/uk/sports-nutrition/post-workout). The body is crying out for a number of different nutrients: protein, carbohydrates and creatine to name a few. An all in one supplement, such as **[Complete All in One™](/uk/products/complete-all-in-one/bble-cai1)**, offers a convenient and cost-effective way of providing your body with all these nutrients and more – immediately after training, when they are needed most!

All in One Supplements FAQS
---------------------------

What is an all in one protein supplement?

An all-in-one protein supplement contains the different proteins your body requires, that contributes to you seeing results from exercise. We often find ourselves making these, putting powder from various pouches into one mega-shake. With these dedicated supplements, we get a wide array of protein, amino acids and carbs in one simple, easy-to-use supplement.

What are the benefits of all in one protein shakes?

Our body cries out for protein as part of the recovery process, along with carbs, amino acids and creatine, amongst others. With all-in-one shakes, you can be sure you’re getting all of these nutrients that contribute to your fitness journey, can improve muscle growth, and replenish your body.

What different ingredients are in all in one protein powders?

All-in-one supplements contain a wide range of ingredients, as you can probably guess from the name. Within this, we try to cover all of the main nutrients your body needs for recovery - foremostly, protein. Alongside this, there’s also carbohydrates and amino acids, all of a high quality, to ensure you get as full a mix as possible of these crucial nutritional components.

Who should take all in one protein powders?

All-in-one protein supplements are suitable for those looking to increase their intake of essential nutrients such as protein, carbohydrates and amino acids, in a high-quality way using premium ingredients, that allows you to avoid excessive calorie intake. Whether you’re bulking up, or just looking to maintain muscle mass, protein powder can contribute to this as part of a healthy and varied diet.

Are all in one protein shakes vegan?

Unfortunately, due to the milk products in our all-in-one protein shakes, vegans are not able to use all of these. However, our Vegan All-In-One might be a suitable alternative - all of the nutritional goodness, with bulk’s signature high-quality ingredient life and premium profile.

What is the shelf life of all in one powders?

All-In-One powders have a long shelf life. Every pouch has an expiry date which you should adhere to, and be careful to follow storage instructions so you’re getting the most out of your supplements.

Is there a difference between all-in-one protein shakes and regular whey protein powders?

Yes, whey protein powder (commonly consisting of whey protein concentrate or whey isolate) is purely protein. Whereas all-in-one supplements tend to contain more ingredients to help promote recovery, such as amino acids and carbohydrates.
