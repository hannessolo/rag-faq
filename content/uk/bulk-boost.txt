Frequently asked questions
--------------------------

* * *

How does Bulk™ Boost work? +

Log in or create an account with us and you can sign up to **Bulk™** Boost by simply purchasing it like you would a product. Place an order valued at £10 or more before 9pm on any day of the week to receive it the next working day. Once you have **Bulk™** Boost added to your account, this delivery option will automatically be applied at checkout.

* * *

Do I need an account? +

Yes, you’ll need an account so that we can automatically apply the **Bulk™** Boost delivery option to your applicable orders. It’s easy to sign up [here](https://www.bulk.com/uk/customer/account/create/).

* * *

What happens after 12 months? +

We’ll notify you when there is 30 days left on your **Bulk™** Boost membership with the option to repurchase. **Bulk™** Boost is not a rolling subscription and we will not direct debit any funding from you.

* * *

I have changed my mind, can I cancel? +

You will be able to cancel before your first order using **Bulk™** Boost delivery.

* * *

Is there anywhere in the UK Bulk™ Boost delivery is not available? +

Yes, there are restrictions to next-working-day deliveries to the post codes mentioned on this page: [UK Delivery Restrictions](https://help.bulk.com/hc/en-gb/articles/208826525-UK-Delivery-Restrictions#h_01JASVZ467T2JC3W7MC2RMW6NR).

* * *

I don’t live in the UK, can I get Bulk™ Boost? +

At this stage **Bulk™** Boost is limited to the UK only.

* * *

What carrier does Bulk™ Boost use? +

**Bulk™** Boost uses Evri.
